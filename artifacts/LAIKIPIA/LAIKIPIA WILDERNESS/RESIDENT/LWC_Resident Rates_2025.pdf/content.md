## T's and C's

- Children under 5 are welcomed by special arrangement
- Photographic groups require a private vehicle
- Leopard-specific trips require a Black leopard-specific vehicle to enable priority at a leopard sighting. This must be booked and paid for in advance.  Single photographers are encouraged to book a Shared vehicle. booking a black leopard vehicle does not guarantee sightings as she is a wild animal. This is only for a priority slot at a sighting where the number of vehicles is limited. Not necessary for all clients.
- No self-drive is permitted once at camp
- Management reserves the right to deny entry
- All residents are required to provide proof of residency
- Min 2 night stay outside of peak season, Min 3 night stay during peak season

- The balance payment of 80% is required 60 days prior to travel

## Cancellations

- Deposits are non-refundable
- 1 month - 60 days before travel, half the full value forfeited
- 0 - 30 days before travel, no refund
