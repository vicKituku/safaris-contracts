## BOOKING TERMS &amp; CONDITIONS  -2025

Bookings are accepted on the understanding that guests have their own comprehensive travel insurance with at least cover for Medical, Repatriation and Cancellation included.

Bookings are confirmed upon receipt of a 20% Non-refundable deposit.

Balance payable in full 45 days prior to arrival.

Room check in is 11:00 AM on day of arrival and check out time is 10:00AM on the day of departure.

## Cancellation Policy:

Cancellations are only effective on receipt of written notification. Upon confirmation:

- Up to 61 days prior to arrival, 100% of monies received is refunded*
- 60-45 days prior to arrival, the 20% deposit of the total cost is retained
- 44-30 days prior to arrival, 50% of the total cost is retained
- 29 days-to arrival date, 100% of the total cost is retained

*Monies refunded are minus applicable bank charges.

## To make a provisional booking:

Please send an email to info@ilngwesi.com. Your booking will be held provisionally for 14 days. The expiry date of the booking will be noted on the reservation summary that you are sent. You are required to confirm the booking prior to the expiry date otherwise it will automatically expire. If you require more time to confirm your booking, the expiry date may be extended once (on request).

## To confirm a booking:

A booking shall only be confirmed upon receipt of the 20% non-refundable deposit. Proof of Comprehensive Travel Insurance is required.

## Please ensure that the booking voucher contains the following information:

- Full Names of ALL guests in the group and ages (especially of children)
- Any special dietary requirements, allergies or other special requests
- Date, time and method of guest's arrival and departure
- Advise if any mobility challenges. (the lodge is located on a hill and those)
- Any special celebrations-birthdays, anniversaries etc.
