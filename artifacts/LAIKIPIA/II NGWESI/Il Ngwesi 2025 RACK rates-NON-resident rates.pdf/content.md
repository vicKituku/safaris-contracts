## Lodge Inclusions: Lodge Inclusions:

-  Full board accommodation,
-  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Afternoon tea and dinner
-  Softdrinks: Water, Tea and Soda
-  Guided bush walks
-  Local game drives within Il Ngwesi conservancy (2 game drives per day )
-  Hiking the Marurui hill for interested guests
-  Bush breakfasts and sundowners(Alcoholic drinks are charged upon consumption)
-  Visit to rhino sanctuary(entry fees included)
-  Visit to cultural manyatta(entry fees included)

## For 3+ nights stay one day visit to Lewa Conservancy is included.

Lodge Exclusions: Conservation fees, transfers, Beading workshop, beauty treatments and all alcoholic drinks.

For 3+ nights stay, a visit to Ngarendare Forest Trust can be conducted on request at an extra fee of $70 per person per drive.

Camping can be supported on request withstaff and security in Il Ngwesi and Ngare Ndare Forest. Camping and conservation fees apply. Camping equipment canbe provided.
