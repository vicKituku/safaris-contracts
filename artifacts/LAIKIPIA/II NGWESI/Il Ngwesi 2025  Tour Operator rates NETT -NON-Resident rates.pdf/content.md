-  Full board accommodation,
-  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Afternoon tea and dinner
-  Soft  drinks: Water, Tea and Soda
-  Guided bush walks
-  Local game drives within Il Ngwesi conservancy (2 game drives per day )
-  Hiking the Marurui hill for interested guests
