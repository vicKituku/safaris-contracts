## AMBOSELI DISCOVERY CAMP

Amboseli Discovery Camp is located a few feet away from Amboseli National Park. We have a million-dollar view of Mt. Kilimanjaro. The camp sits in the open savannah with elephants and many African wildlife strolling through our watering hole located a few feet away from our elevated guest lounge.

We are in a wildlife corridor frequently used by elephants moving from the park to Kitenden wildlife sanctuary. The newly built luxury camp, made of Hemingway style Safari tents, allows guests to stay far away from the tourists' crowds. We have everything you desire for peace and tranquility.

## Accommodation

Each safari tent has a private bathroom and free toiletries. Wi-Fi and private parking are available for free. The camp offers 8 Hemingway-style Safari tents each with its own private veranda with stunning mountain view. Each tent is beautifully furnished; it's simple and charming at the same time. Each tent is equipped with mosquito nets, small table, chairs, lighting (powered with solar panels) flush toilets, and hot water.

The entire camp sits in the open savannah dotted with indigenous acacia trees that provide a cool haven from the equatorial heat. Whether you dreamed of walking with giraffes across the African landscape, Amboseli Discovery Camp offer the exclusive experience.

ACTIVITIES INCLUDES (OPTIONAL WITH SURCHARGE): Full day Safari Excursion to Amboseli National Park. Walking Safari lead by Maasai warriors, bead -making lessons facilitated by Maasai Women Co-operatives, a tour to local Maasai villages. Guests will also have option to go on a bird watching safari with English speaking guides. Also balloon ride is available upon request. Customer satisfaction is guaranteed .
