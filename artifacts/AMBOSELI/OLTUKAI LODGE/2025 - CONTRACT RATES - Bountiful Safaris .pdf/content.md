## CHILD POLICY

- 0 - 1.99 Years -Infant ,2 -11.99 Years ,Children, Above 12 years adults
- a) One child in a single room -Single room rate will apply
- b) Two children in a room, the charge is equivalent to a single rate. i.e 75 % of the double rate.
- c) Three children in a room the rate will be a single plus the child rate (equivalent to a full double rate.)
- d) Four children in a room; the charge will be equivalent to a full twin room
- e) When we have one adult and one child in a room; charged as a single and a Child sharing.
- f) Adult sharing with two children, the rate charged will be the equivalent to a full twin room.
- g) One adult sharing a room with three children; the rate is twin plus child sharing.
- h) Two adults sharing with one child the rate to be charged as a twin plus child sharing rate i.e 50 % of an Adult in double
