## Overview:

- Tulia Amboseli Safari Camp has: Mountainview Superb Tents - 9 Double /Twin Tents, and 1 Family tent (Max. of 6) Mountainview Deluxe Tents w/Private Plunge Pools - 10 Double /Twin Tents
- All rates are quoted in USD, and the adult sharing rates are a per person sharing (PPS) rate.
- Rates are inclusive of all statutory taxes.
- All quoted rates are NETT and are non-commissionable.
- All EXTRA services are quoted on NETT basis.
- Full Board rates include breakfast, lunch, dinner, and in-room water.
- Game Package rates include breakfast, lunch, dinner, shared 4 x 4 open-sided Safari Trucks, walking safari, airstrip transfers, Amboseli Park Kimana gate transfers, and soft drinks and water while on safari.
- Single Room policy -We allow a maximum of TWO Single rooms per booking.

## Child Policy:

- Child rate is applicable to children aged 5 - 11.9 years sharing with one or two adults.
- Children under 5 years stay free (excluding park fees).
- Children staying in their own room pay 75% of the room rate.
