## PRODUCT INCLUSIONS :

Flights operate 7 days a week including all holidays at sunrise, subject to weather conditions. Approx. 1-hour Hot Air Balloon Safari (ranges from 45 -90 minutes). 4 -5 hours from pick-up to return. Chefprepared 'Made to Order' hot breakfast . Traditional Champagne Celebration. In-flight commentary and highlights by expert Captains, giving passengers insights on the local ecosystem, Mt. Kilimanjaro, the Amboseli National Park, local game in the area, hot air ballooning history and principals including meteorology, altitude, navigation, equipment, and life in the bush. Return transportation Game drive to the Balloon Camp and on return to the Camp or Lodge.

Commemorative Flight Ascension Certificate, signed by the Captain.

## AMENDMENT AND CANCELLATION POLICY :

If we cancel the flight, you will be entitled to a full refund, unless rescheduled for another day. If you cancel within:

- a. 14-days prior to flight date -No fees charged.
- b. 14 -7 days prior -50% charged.
- c. 6 days -48 hours -75% charged.
- d. Less than 48 hours -100% charged.
- e. No show -100% charged.

Large Groups of passengers may be subject to different cancellation conditions.

For Group and FOC policies, please contact the Kilimanjaro Balloon Safaris team.

Hot Air Ballooning is a weather dependant activity, so if the balloon flight is cancelled due to inclement weather, passengers can either reschedule, or receive a full refund.
