## Flight Details

At each camp or lodge, we have a pre-determined Meeting Point for pick-up of your pre-booked guests. The time and location will be reconfirmed the afternoon before the flight with the guests or their guide. Meeting time varies depending on the Camp or Lodge and may begin as early as 4:15am as we aim to have all guests at the Balloon Camp prior to 5:30am.

Upon arrival,  passengers  will  visit  and  check-in  at  the  reception  desk  and  complete Passenger Information Waivers. Morning beverages and fresh cookies are available in the Guest Pavilion as well as male and female washrooms, WiFi and USB chargers.

A short safety briefing with the Captain of the Balloon precedes the flight on the launch field and Guests will enjoy photo opportunities while the balloon is inflated. In flight, the guests will experience a spectacular 360-degree view of the Amboseli ecosystem, Mt Kilimanjaro, Amboseli National Park, local Maasai Manyattas and plentiful game. Flights generally last about 1-hour, but actual flight time can vary from 45-minutes to 1 ½ hours, depending on the weather conditions of the day. Flight length, direction or game viewed cannot be guaranteed on a given day as we are at the mercy of Mother Nature.

Post-flight, the guests are returned to the Camp to partake in a Champagne Celebration with their Captain and reminisce about their flight. A freshly prepared 'made to order' full hot breakfast is served inclusive of fresh fruits, pastries, cereals, yoghurt, coffee, a selection of teas, hot chocolate and juices. Washrooms, WiFi and USB chargers are available for guest convenience and comfort. Prior to returning guests to their camp, all guests are presented with a Commemorative Flight Ascension Certificate, signed by their Captain, as a memento of their Kilimanjaro Balloon Safari experience.

## What to Wear

Hot Air Ballooning is an interactive experience so it is best to wear clothing that can get dusty or dirty and is suitable for hiking:

- Some mornings can be quite cool so layering clothing is recommended so it can be removed as the morning warms
- The hot air balloon burners produce radiant heat, so it is recommended to wear a hat. The burners can sometimes drip water mixed with carbon. KBS will not be responsible for clothing damaged or stained
- Launch and landing can be in rough terrain. Closed toe shoes are recommended for all guests and high-heeled shoes or open toe shoes are NOT suitable.
- Bring a camera to capture stunning photos of the flight!
