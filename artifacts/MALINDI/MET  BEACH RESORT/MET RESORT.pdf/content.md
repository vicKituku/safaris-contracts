## METBEACHRESORTMALINDI

Met Beach Resort Malindi is an amazing boutique Resort with a total of 11 deluxe rooms, perched on a natural magnificently Manicured garden giving you a panoramic view of the Malindi Marine Park Beach just 5 km away from the ancient Malindi Town.

The Resort offers Accommodation in our spacious rooms whereby 6 rooms are sea view facing and 5 rooms are garden view facing. Wealso have a restaurant that is both Sea and pool facing with a variety of mouthwatering cuisine of American, Italian and African Swahili. From Malindi town, Met Beach Resort Malindi is located off Casuarina Road, End of Marine Park Road and Opposite The Navy Base.
