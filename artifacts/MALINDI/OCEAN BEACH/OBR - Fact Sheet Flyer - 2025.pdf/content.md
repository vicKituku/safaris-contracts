## LOCATION

Ocean Beach Resort epitomizes luxury with its superior deluxe 5-star accommodations, offering a return to the essence of hospitality. Its distinctive tropical early 20thcentury style, characterised by wooden roofs and elegant floors, sets it apart.

Conveniently located, the resort is accessible from Moi International Airport in Mombasa (120km) and Malindi Airport in Malindi (5.7km).

The climate at Ocean Beach Resort is characterised by hot days averaging 33 degrees Celsius and warm nights averaging 24 degrees Celsius. With a tropical climate and high humidity levels, the resort experiences conventional rains during May/June and September/October.
