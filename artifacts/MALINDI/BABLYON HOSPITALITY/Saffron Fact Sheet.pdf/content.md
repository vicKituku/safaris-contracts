## ABOUT

Welcome to Saffron Garden, one of the most magical place on Earth.

Saffron Garden is a 5-acre oasis where nature, gastronomy, art and tradition come together in a unique way. It is an eclectic and multidisciplinary space that has become the true meeting point for all travellers on the Kenyan Coast.

## ACCOMODATION

Enjoy the most soothing and relaxing atmosphere by staying in one of the 15 rooms. Spaces designed for those who want to live a truly special experience without giving up maximum comfort.

## RESTAURANT

Join us for an unforgettable culinary journey in a truly stunning setting. Savor our daily Kenyan dishes with authentic flavors, or indulge in our exquisite international menu, thoughtfully curated from our travels around the world.

## MALINDI ARTISAN MARKET

Discover MAM@Saffron, the new Malindi Artisan Market and meet the local masters of craft with a unique Hanging Museum where all our trees become the stage for local artists.

## LOCATION

Nestled in a quiet setting right in the center of Malindi, our location offers peaceful accommodation with convenient access to shopping malls, banks, and the airport.
