## 1. Agreement Overview

This agreement outlines the terms and conditions under which the Tour Operator may market and sell accommodation and activities at O'Leleshwa Retreat to its clients at contracted STO rates.

## Period Contracted

a.

The present agreement is made for hotel accommodation rates and related services &amp; facilities to be provided at the Hotel for the period of 1 ST  OCTOBER 2025 TO 31 ST  DECEMBER 2026 (herein referred to as the 'Validity of Contract').

b.

Notwithstanding the above, if the Property is closed permanently (subject to the situation) and/or the management of the Property, related or associated companies ceases, the present agreement shall be terminated automatically and as of right as from the effective date of the said permanent closure or cessation of the management, as the case may be. This is not applicable in the case of temporary closure. The Property will however have to give to the Key Account written notice of 90 days prior or as soon as possible, to the effective date of such permanent or temporary closure, or cessation of management.

- i. For contract purposes, the Property defines Children/Teens as follows: Children of all ages are welcome.
