O'Leleshwa Retreat Announces Official Opening in Naivasha, Kenya A New Chapter in Luxury and Community-Driven Hospitality

Naivasha, Kenya -O'Leleshwa Retreat, an exclusive new luxury property nestled in the scenic beauty of Naivasha, is set to officially welcome guests from 1st November 2025 . Managed by Lodgistics Professional Hospitality Solutions and represented in sales by Poised Solutions , O'Leleshwa Retreat is a shining example of industry collaboration, combining expert management, innovative sales strategies, and a deep commitment to community empowerment. The culinary experience is the cornerstone of the offering, where guests are set to experience exceptional cuisine, with a number of the fresh ingredients being grown on the property by the team.

The retreat features three distinct villas , each thoughtfully designed for comfort, privacy, and a sense of connection to nature:

- O'Lorien Villa -3 en suite bedrooms with a shared lounge and kitchenette
- O'Leleshwa Villa -4 en suite bedrooms with a shared lounge and kitchenette
- O'Lerai Villa -2 en suite bedrooms with a shared lounge and kitchenette

O'Leleshwa is centrally located in the Great Rift Valley, making it the ideal luxury destination from which to explore the myriad of activities and experiences the region has to offer. Exceptional Golf facilities, cycling, walking, boat rides, local game conservancies and big 5 experiences, mountain and forest hikes, cultural tours, rose farm visits and a variety of other excursions are easy to experience.

## A property with a purpose

From its inception, O'Leleshwa Retreat has been more than just a luxury escape-it's a catalyst for local empowerment. A large portion of the building team comprised unskilled and inexperienced youth from neighbouring communities. Throughout the construction process, these individuals learned valuable building skills, site management, and professional work methodologies.

This commitment to capacity building extends beyond the build. Selected youth are now part of upskilling and educational programmes , including a dedicated vocational hospitality and property maintenance training initiative . The goal: to equip as many local young people as possible with skills they can take to the wider Kenyan job market, fostering both individual opportunity and community growth.

## Target markets and industry insights

O'Leleshwa's sales and marketing strategy will initially focus on key international and regional source markets, recognising Kenya's strong appeal globally. According to Kenya Tourism Board statistics, the USA leads as the largest non-African source market, with 209,360 visitors in 2022 . While regional tourism numbers remain significant, a higher percentage of travellers from other continents arrive for leisure purposes, presenting a prime opportunity for O'Leleshwa to attract high-value holiday travellers.
