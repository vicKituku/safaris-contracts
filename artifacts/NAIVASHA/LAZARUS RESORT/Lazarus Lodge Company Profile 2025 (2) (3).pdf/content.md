## GEO LOCATION AND SURROUNDINGS

Lake Naivasha Lazarus Lodge is nestled along Moi South Lake Road, Naivasha, oering guests breathtaking views of Lake Naivasha and its serene natural surroundings. The lodge provides a peaceful escapeinto nature, where guests can enjoy the tranquility of the lake, the vibrant birdlife and the lush greenery of the region.

Its prime location makes it an ideal base for exploring Naivasha's attractions, including nearby national parks, game sanctuaries and scenic hiking destinations. Visitors can immerse themselves in the area's rich biodiversity, from gentle boat rides on the lake to guided excursions that showcase the beauty and wildlife of this iconic part of Kenya.

## Our surroundings include:

- Natural Attractions: Lake Naivasha, home to hippos and over 400 bird species; near Hell's Gate National Park.
- Proximity: 10-15 minutes from Naivasha town; close to Crater Lake Game Sanctuary.
- Climate: Mild temperatures (15-25°C), with seasonal flamingo migrations.
- Technical Details: GPS: -0.7667° S, 36.4333° E; Elevation: 1,890 meters. The lodge oers secure parking and shuttle services for seamless access.
