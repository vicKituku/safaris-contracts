## Payment Terms

Booking will only be confirmed on receipt of 30% deposit.  Full balance of payment is required 60 days prior to clients' arrival. A confirmation voucher will only be issued on receipt of 100% payment.  La Pieve Limited may at its discretion not honor a booking if payment has not been recived on the payment schedule.

## CANCELLATION POLICY

Accomodation cancelled or reduced in number between 65 days &amp; 40 days -30% total cost payable Accomodation cancelled or reduced in number between 39 days and 30 days -50% total cost payable Accomodation cancelled or reduced in number between 29 days &amp; no show -100% total cost payable 30% nonrefundable deposit must be made to confirm booking
