## INTRODUCTION TO OUR HOTEL SERVICES

ESERIANI RESORT is one of the hotels under Eseriani Hotels and Resorts. We officially opened this Facility in March 2020. Eseriani Resort is based in Naivasha, within the Rift valley and situated along Moi South Lake Road.

With 46 well designed and tastefully furnished accommodation rooms, 6 meeting rooms, 2 Boardrooms, and one main hall, Urban restaurant and Bar/lounge, this Resort is bound to meet the needs of every guest whether travelling for business or  leisure.
