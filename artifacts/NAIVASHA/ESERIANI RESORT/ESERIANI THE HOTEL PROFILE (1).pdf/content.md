## Who we are

Established in December 2013, Eseriani, which means 'Place of peace' in the Maasai language, is a boutique hotel situated in Naivasha within the tranquil Rift Valley.

With 20 modern well designed rooms, conference rooms with modern state of the art facilities and an urban restaurant, the Hotel is bound to meet the needs of every guest, whether on business, leisure or travel.
