## INTRODUCTION TO OSOTUA LUXURY RESORT NAIVASHA

Osotua Luxury Resort, Naivasha is a welcoming Oasis that provides a stylish escape in serene seclusion. The resort is located along Moi South Lake Road, Naivasha with a lake front view. The resort offers a selection of 30 Spacious Studio apartments. The Studio Apartments are tastefully furnished to offer the  highest standards of comfort and value at affordable rates. All studios have fully furnished equipment including a bedside table, tea making facilities, high speed internet connectivity and smart TVs.

Every part of the hotel has been designed with the guests' comfort in mind.  The terrace and dining areas have spectacular views of both the lake and the pool. Each uniquely -presented Studios Room is accommodated in its own stand-out modular unit with uninterrupted views and spacious seating area. Different bird species echo the air, Colobus Monkeys dance on trees.

Other activities that you may enjoy include a boat ride round Lake Naivasha; spotting Hippo families as well as the Crescent Island Sanctuary for connectivity with nature amongst them Giraffes, Zebras, etc. You can also enjoy excursions to Hell's Gate National Park, hiking on Mount Longonot, a visit to Olkaria Natural Hot Spa, a tour to Lake Nakuru National Park and adventure in Kereita Forest for Zip lining among others.

## Conference facilities

Our fully equipped conference rooms accommodate up to 40 Pax. The Conference halls are ideal for Seminars, Workshops and Executive meetings. The rates are competitively priced and packaged to meet your business or social needs. Our skilled dedicated staff will ensure that the outcome of your event is a success and memorable. Meals are consistently varied with production of appetizing culinary delights delectable to taste. Excellent and Quality Service are the ethos of Osotua
