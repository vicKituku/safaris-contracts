## Welcome to Lantana Galu Beach

Nestled on one of Africa's and Kenya's most stunning beaches, adjacent to Diani Beachvoted Africa's Leading Beach Destination at the World Travel Awards 2024 and the Top Beach in Kenya by TripAdvisor in 2023 -Lantana Galu Beach is the ultimate beach escape .

Set within expansive tropical gardens, our resort features a range of spacious, elegantly designed and Swahili inspired beachfront Suites &amp; Villas , catering to families, couples and groups alike, offering a perfect blend of comfort and luxury.

Lantana Galu Beach upholds international 5-star standards, having earned numerous TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice Awards , the latest being in 2025 together with other accolades over the years including LuxLife -Most Tranquil Resort, Diani Beach 2023 and the World Luxury Hotel Awards -East Africa Regional Winner 2019: Luxury Family Beach Resort.

Our guests are consistently impressed by our exceptional, personalised service . At Lantana Galu Beach, we pride ourselves on providing a level of hospitality that ensures every stay is unforgettable.

## Accommodation at Lantana Galu Beach

All the Suites &amp; Villas at Lantana Galu Beach include a dining area to seat 6 or 8 people, lounge with satellite TV, ceiling fans, air-conditioning and mosquito nets in the bedrooms, dining and lounge terrace, fully equipped kitchen with kettle, cooker, fridge/freezer, microwave, cutlery, crockery and glassware, laundry room with iron, washing machine and drying area.

## Rates &amp; Booking Terms &amp; Conditions

Our rates include accommodation and meals (where indicated), housekeeping once a day, evening turn-down service, satellite TV, Wi-Fi and all government taxes and levies.

A deposit of 50% is required to confirm a booking, full payment is due 30 days prior to arrival. Deposits are non-refundable. Cancellations made 35-25 days before arrival incur a 75% cancellation fee. Cancellations made less than 25 days before arrival incur a 100% cancellation fee.

## Le Café at Lantana Galu Beach -Beachside Restaurant &amp; Bar

With stunning views of the beach and pool, the restaurant offers a relaxed yet unforgettable dining experience. Choose from the ocean breeze by the beach, poolside, or our vibrant garden. Our à la carte and Room Service menus feature freshly brewed coffee, creamy ice creams, wines, and Procera gin cocktails. Enjoy our standout dishes: ⬧ Fresh Catch of the Day ⬧ Swahili Coconut Prawn Curry ⬧ Grilled Specialties &amp; Wood-Fired Pizzas ⬧ Healthy Kids' Menu ⬧ Tempting Desserts!

## Reception &amp; Concierge Services

The Reception runs on a 24-hour basis. Our Receptionists can advise on and book excursions, restaurants, water sport activities and taxis on behalf of guests. They are also a guest's first point of contact for any additional requirements or requests during their stay at Lantana Galu Beach.

## Fitness &amp; Massage

The gym, comprising a large airy room with mirrors is fully equipped with the latest fitness machines. The gym is open all day for residents to use freely. Adjacent to the gym is the 5enses massage &amp; beauty studio offering relaxing massages, treatments and therapies.

## The Lantana Shop

In addition to offering convenience sundry items, the shop also stocks a variety of trendy costal wear, accessories and artwork by local artists.

## Amenities &amp; Facilities at Lantana Galu Beach

## Business Centre

For printing, scanning and photocopying services in addition to access to a computer.

## Swimming Pools with Paddling Pools

There are two leisure pools each with a paddling pool for children: the beachside swimming pool and the garden swimming pool with sun beds and fresh pool towels provided every day.

## Water Sports &amp; Activities

Lantana Galu Beach is in close proximity to Diani's water sport centres offering: kite surfing, diving, snorkelling, wind surfing, deep sea fishing, dolphin safaris, jet skiing. Activities can be booked via the Reception.

## Baby Cots &amp; Highchairs

These can be provided for children at no extra cost upon request and subject to availability.

## Honeymoon Package

Our Honeymoon Package includes a 30-minute spa treatment for 2, a seafood dinner for 2 at Le Café , our beachside restaurant, with a bottle of sparkling wine and flowers in the unit. Minimum 3-night stay or 7 nights over Peak Season. Proof of marriage required.

## Weddings &amp; Celebrations

For up to 300 guests, we offer bespoke weddings and party packages with tailor-made menus and setup backed by our team of experienced and dedicated staff renowned for their exemplary service.

## We are Plastic Free

Lantana Galu Beach has pledged to support the UN Environment's efforts to create a plastic free coastline . As such we no longer provide plastic straws, water is served in glass bottles and our in-unit soaps, shampoos and conditioner are provided in metal, refillable bottles.
