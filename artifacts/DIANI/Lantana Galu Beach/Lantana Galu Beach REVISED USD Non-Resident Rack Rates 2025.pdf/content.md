## Reception &amp; Concierge Services

At Lantana Galu Beach, our staff is there to make your stay as enjoyable as possible. Our reception is open 24 hours a day and offers a concierge service for bookings of a wide range of services including water sports, excursions, taxis, tuk-tuks and restaurants.

## Le Café Diani Restaurant &amp; Bar | Room Service

Situated by the main swimming pool, relax with stunning views of the beach and all-day service at the restaurant, in the gardens or by the swimming pool. Enjoy a divers à la carte and in-house Room Service Menu .

## Convenience Shop

Located by Reception, the shop sells a variety of sundry grocery items that can also be ordered in advance. In addition, you will find an array of other local and international items.

## Private Chef

A private chef can be sourced for guests upon request depending on availability. Charges for the chef are usually settled directly between guests and the chef.

## Weddings &amp; Celebrations

For up to 300 guests, we can cater for weddings and parties with tailor-made menus backed by our team of experienced and dedicated staff renowned for offering exemplary service.

## Honeymoon Package

Our Honeymoon Package includes a 30 minute spa treatment for 2, a seafood dinner for 2 at Le Café with a bottle of sparkling wine and flowers in your suite or villa. Minimum 3 nights' stay or 7 nights over Peak Season. Proof of marriage required.

## Baby Cots &amp; High Chairs

Baby cots and high chairs for children can be provided at no extra charge upon request and subject to availability.

## Beach Side &amp; Garden Swimming Pools

The main leisure pool, a step away from the beach, offers the ideal space for sunbathing, relaxation and leisure which also includes a paddling pool for children. The garden swimming pool offers a quieter area to sunbathe and relax by the water and includes a paddling pool for children.

## Fitness &amp; Massage

The Lantana Gym , comprising a large airy room with light parquet floor and mirrors is fully equipped with the latest fitness machines. The gym is open all day for residents to use freely. Adjacent to the gym is the massage &amp; beauty studio offering relaxing treatments and therapies to include: holistic massages, aromatherapy facials, nail therapies, hairdressing and much more.

## Business Centre

Located adjacent to the reception, the business centre offers printing, scanning, photocopying and an internet connection.

## Barbeques

Barbeque sets can be provided at no extra charge upon request and subject to availability (villas and plunge pool suites only).

## WiFi

There are high-speed wireless internet connections which can be accessed at no extra charge throughout the property.

## Water Sports, Activities &amp; Excursions

Lantana Galu Beach is within close proximity to Diani's water sport centres. Activities can be booked via reception to include: diving, snorkelling, kite surfing, windsurfing, glass bottom boat, deep sea fishing, dolphin safaris, jet skiing, quad biking, cycling tours...
