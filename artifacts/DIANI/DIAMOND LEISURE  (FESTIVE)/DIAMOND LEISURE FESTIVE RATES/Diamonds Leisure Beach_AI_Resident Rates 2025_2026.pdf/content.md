## ROOMS OCCUPANCY

Maximum occupancy: 3 adults, or 2 adults with 1 child Extra beds available upon request: Two-Bedroom Suite: up to 2 Four-Bedroom Oasis Villa: up to 4 Six-Bedroom Oasis Villa: up to 6 Interconnecting rooms available on request Children aged 0-5.99 years enjoy complimentary stay and dining when sharing with adults

- In-room mini fridge stocked with water, soft drinks,
- Breakfast, lunch, and dinner served at the Diani Restaurant
- Sunday brunch available weekly at Coral Beach Restaurant &amp; Bar (at a charge)
- A selection of beverages including soft drinks, water, coffee, tea, local beer, house wine by the glass, selected spirits, and cocktails, served from 10:00 to 24:00 across our bars. Bahari Bar: 10:00 - 24:00
- Wi-Fi in rooms and public areas
- Fitness and gymnastic activities, table tennis, billiards, beach volleyball, and non-motorized water sports including stand-up paddle and kayaks
- Daily animation, evening entertainment, and local cultural shows
- Simba Kids Club and Junior Club
- Sun loungers, umbrellas, and complimentary towels by the pool and on the beach
- Special festive dining programs during school holidays, Mother's Day, Father's Day, Easter, Christmas, and New Year
