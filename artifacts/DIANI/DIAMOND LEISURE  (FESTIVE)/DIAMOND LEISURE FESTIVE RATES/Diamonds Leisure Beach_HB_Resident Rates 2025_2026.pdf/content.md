## TERMS &amp; CONDITIONS

Rates are subject to availability at time of booking. | Applicable to East African residents only. | All prices in Ksh

## ROOMS OCCUPANCY

Maximum occupancy: 3 adults, or 2 adults with 1 child Extra beds available upon request: Two-Bedroom Suite: up to 2 Four-Bedroom Oasis Villa: up to 4 Six-Bedroom Oasis Villa: up to 6 Interconnecting rooms available on request Children aged 0-5.99 years enjoy complimentary stay and dining when sharing with adults

## HALF BOARD PACKAGE

- Welcome Drink upon arrival
- Coffee and tea-making facilities in all rooms
- Breakfast and Dinner served at the Diani Restaurant
- Selected beverages during meals
- Wi-Fi access in all rooms and public areas
- Use of resort facilities, including: Table tennis, billiards,beach volleyball, Fitness &amp; gymnastic activities Non-motorized water sports: stand-up paddleboard (SUP) and kayaks
- Simba Kids and Junior Club
- Daily pool and beach towels Sun loungers and umbrellas at the pool and beach
- Evening entertainment with soft animation and live local shows
- Access to all bars and restaurants (drinks and meals outside the half-board package charged separately): Bahari Bar : 10:00-24:00 Coral Beach Bar : 10:00-18:00 (meals &amp; snacks available at additional charge) Maweni Pool Bar : 10:00-18:00 Baobab Swim-Up Bar : 10:00-18:00 Lobby Bar : 10:00-23:00 (Afternoon tea served from 16:00-17:00) Frangipane Pizzeria Restaurant (Pizza dinner available at an additional charge)
- Sunday brunch at Coral Beach Restaurant &amp; Bar (surcharge applies)
