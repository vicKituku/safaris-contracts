## BOOKING POLICY

* All booking requests will be allocated a Reservation Number
* A Performa invoice will be issued showing accommodation charges for the booking.
* a 50% deposit will be requested and is payable within two weeks of the issue date on the proforma. During this period, the booking is held in the system as provisional.
* Upon receipt of the deposit, the booking is allocated a confirmed status and charted.
* If the deposit is not received, the reservation is automatically revoked and the rooms become available for resell.
* Balance of payment is expected before guest arrival - preferably two weeks before arrival.
* cancellation policy
- 0-14 days prior to arrival date, 100% chargeable

15-35 days prior to arrival date, 50% chargeable
