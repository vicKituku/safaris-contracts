## Meal Plan Inclusions:

## Bed and Breakfast (BB)

Accommodation and Breakfast Daily.

## Half Board (HB)

Accommodation, Breakfast, High Tea (16h00) and Dinner daily.

## Full Board (FB)

Accommodation, Breakfast, High Tea (16h00), Lunch and Dinner daily.

** All guest on Half Board and Full Board packages are entitled to 1 a la carte dinner in Zanzibar Restaurant, Spice Route and Barharini Restaurant in their stay.

** Additional (a la carte) dinners will be charged the applicable a la carte menu price.

## Child Policy before December 2025

4 to 12 years sharing with adults in superior or executive suite HB pays $50 per child per night 4 to 12 years sharing with adults in superior or executive suite FB pays $65 per child per night Children sharing in their own room pays 85% of the adult room rate per night
