## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

## Child Policy

- Child sharing (6 -12 years old) Maximum 1 child with 2 adults: As per rate grid
- Child sharing (6 -12 years old) Maximum 1 child with 1 adults = Double Room rate
- 0-5 years old sharing (maximum 2 children): FREE
- 6-12 years old in own room (max. 2 children) -75% of adult double rate
- 12 years old and above = full adult rate

## Cancellation Policy

- All booking cancellations must be advised in writing to the reservations team: rsv@dianireef.com.
- No refund will be issued for any cancellation made within 7 days before the check-in date.
- A 50% refund will be issued if cancellation is made between 8-30 days before check in date.
- A full refund for cancellation effected 31 days and above before check-in date.
- During festive season, any cancellation made within 31 days of arrival will incur 100% charge of the entire booking.

## Minimum Stay

- Required minimum stay of 3 nights during festive season.
- Check out will not be permitted on 24th and 31st Dec 2025.

## Payment Policy

- The payment must be received 7 days prior the arrival/check-in date.
- Bookings not secured by the appropriate deposit will be released automatically and without prior notification.
- All payments made by cheque must be cleared before the arrival/check-in date. In the event, a cheque is returned by the bank as "unpaid" for any reason the agent shall replace the payment by cash and additionally agrees to pay the returned cheque fee thereof.
- The agent agrees to pay any costs, including legal fees, court costs, and any other expenses incurred by Diani Reef Beach Resort in the collection of unpaid invoices, returned cheques, or default payment.
