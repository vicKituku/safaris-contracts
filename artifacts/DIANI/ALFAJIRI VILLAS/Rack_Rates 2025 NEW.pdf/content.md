## NOTES

- Rates are applicable from 7th January 2025 up to 6th January 2026.
- Rates are in USD inclusive of all taxes.
- Minimum stay 3 nights.
- Check-in time is 11h00.
- Check-out time is 10h00.
- Clients leaving Alfajiri after lunch, no later than 15h00 will have their villa on exclusive use with all services included at 30% of the daily rate.
- Clients leaving Alfajiri after 15h00 will have their villa on exclusive use and all services included at 100% of the daily rate.
- Located only a 5 minute drive from Ukunda airstrip in Diani.
- Transfer from Mombasa airport $ 200 by Alfajiri SUV vehicles, maximum 4 passengers.

## Alfajiri Foundation

- Each guest will contribute a donation of $10 per adult and per teenager per night and $5 per child per night to the Alfajiri Foundation.
- All donations will support marine conservation organisations and local orphanages.

## Terms of Payment

- A 25% non-refundable deposit of the cost of accommodation is required to hold and confirm bookings.
- The remaining balance is due 60 days prior to the arrival date.

## Cancellation Policy

- Cancellation should be in writing and will result in the loss of the deposit.
- If cancellation occurs within 60-31days before arrival, 50% of the cost of accommodation will be charged.
- If cancellation occurs within 30 days of the arrival date, 100% of the cost of accommodation will be charged.
- Guests should have a travel insurance policy covering deposit paid to confirm reservation.

## Insurance

Clients must take out comprehensive insurance cover before travel for cancellation, medical expense, personal baggage and money.
