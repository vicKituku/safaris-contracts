## INTRODUCTION LETTER

Warm greetings from Comfort Gardens Nanyuki.

Allow me to introduce COMFORT GARDENS HOTEL NANYUKI to you. It's an ideal setting for retreats, business meetings, team-building activities, conferences, and gatherings.

The ambience at Comfort Gardens will inspire you to engage in activities that require a tranquil setting. This exclusive establishment offers personalized accommodations and services in a comfortable and welcoming setting, with a distinct character and charm.

Located on the plain of Mount Kenya, right at the Ol Pejeta Conservancy's entrance-Rongai gate. It's a

15km drive from town and 3 hours' drive from Nairobi city.

All rooms are elegantly furnished and serviced, with a spacious lounge and balcony overlooking the OL Pejeta conservancy, complimentary tea and coffee, connected to DSTV and free Wi-Fi.

The cottages are fully equipped with a gas cooker, fridge, microwave, electric kettle, and a fire place. Our on-site restaurant offers a wide range of delectable cuisines from all over the world.
