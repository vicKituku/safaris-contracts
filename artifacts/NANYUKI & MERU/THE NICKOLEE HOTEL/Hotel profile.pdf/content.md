## ABOUT US

Welcome to Tha Nickolee Hotel, a first-of-its-kind 4-Star luxury hotel in Nanyuki redefining the art of hospitality and transcending the ordinary.

We strive to create a memorable experience that's both comfortable and enriching. We invite you to come, relax, and enjoy everything our hotel has to offer.

Tha Nickolee Hotel embodies a refined elegance that whispers luxe in every detail.

This exquisite gem boasts 60 thoughtfully designed rooms and 8 suites, Conferencing, Health &amp; fitness centre and Spa, merging artistry with comfort to create an ambiance that feels both inviting and distinctive.

Our dining options present an inventive mix of local and international cuisine, designed to surprise and inspire, set against a backdrop of understated sophistication.

At Tha Nickolee Hotel, every moment is an invitation to wander, indulge, and experience the extraordinary, all while reveling in an atmosphere that captivates the spirit.

A stay at Tha Nickolee Hotel is not just a getaway, it's an exhilarating journey into comfort and sophistication.
