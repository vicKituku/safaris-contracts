## Karibu Kambi Mstuni Resort

Mount Kenya region has a reputation for innovative resorts, filled with the luxuries of modern living, the allure of the wild, and amazing adventures. One of the most recognizable resorts in the region is Kambi Mstuni.

The resort, whose name means 'forest camp' in Kiswahili, is Laikipia's gem. Located near the Aberdare National Park, Mount Kenya National Park, Thomson's Falls, Lake Ol' Bolossat, and Solio Ranch, it offers guests unbounded scenic views and unparalleled experiences. It also offers nutritious dining options that will delight your taste buds, top-notch hospitality facilities, and professional staff to cater to your every need, whether you are staying for business or pleasure.

Kambi Mstuni Resort's mission is to enrich people's lives with pleasant vacation experiences and memories. To achieve this, the resort has a lot of extended services, amenities, and special offers for every guest. Their offerings include boat rides, horse riding, game drives, camping, conference rooms, photography, nature trail walks, and wedding grounds. Horse riding is suitable for both beginners and experienced riders as the horses are highly trained.

The nature trail walks give you a chance to see various bird species and wild animals. And if you need to create a bonding experience, a boat trip will create priceless memories - think about watching a sunset on the water with a loved one, best friend, or soul mate. Through all these activities, you will feel the sense of stillness and space, and the vastness and majesty of nature.

But the resort is more than a place to rest your head and stow your stuff in between sightseeing excursions. Its high-class rooms and suites offer breathtaking views and unmatched comfort. There are triple rooms, superior double rooms, classic double rooms, and standard single rooms with complete privacy.
