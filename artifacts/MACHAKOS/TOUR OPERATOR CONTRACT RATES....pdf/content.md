## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

## RESERVATIONS

All reservations must be entered into in writing (letter/fax/email/booking voucher/ LPO).

The Weekend rate applies on Friday, Saturday and Sunday nights only. Not applicable to block booked rooms.

Reservations can be made by phone however this must be followed with an LPO, voucher or official letterhead with the authorizing signature. Except for manifest fraud, all documents appearing to have been signed by an authorized signatory shall bind the Tour Operator unless otherwise advised to the hotel and will take effect from the time of notification.

All reservations by the Tour Operator with detailed rooming list should be received by the hotel's Reservations Office prior to guest's arrival.

Confirmation of accommodation by the hotel will be made to you in writing giving a confirmation number and details of the confirmation. The hotel reserves the right not to honour bookings not confirmed in this format.

In the event that the details of the confirmation letter are incorrect, please amend the errors in writing and inform the hotel as soon as possible. The hotel will not be held liable if these errors are not amended in writing at least 72-hours prior to arrival.

Payment default shall constituter cause for credit termination. Failure to replace a returned cheque(s) shall constitute default and the hotel reserves the right to engage a debt collector and or to pursue legal recourse.

The Operator agrees to pay any and all expenses incurred by the hotel in the collection of unpaid invoices and/or returned cheques.
