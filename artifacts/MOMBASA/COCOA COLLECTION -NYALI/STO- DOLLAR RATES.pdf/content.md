## RESERVATION PROCEDURE

Reservations  will  be  made  when  received  via  official  email  with  an  LPO,  Voucher  or  Letterhead  with  the authorizing signature. Except for manifest fraud, all documents appearing to have been signed by an authorized signatory shall bind the Company unless otherwise advised to The Cocoa Collection Hotels and will take effect from the time of notification. All reservations by the company with detailed rooming list should be received by The Cocoa Collection Hotels sales/reservations office prior to guest's arriv al.

## GUARANTEES / DEPOSITS

All reservations shall be guaranteed by cash, cheque, credit card or interbank transfer in such amount as required by The Cocoa Collection Hotels. Non-guaranteed bookings are subject to release by The Cocoa Collection Hotels Nairobi at 18.00hrs on the day of arrival without prior notice.

## ARRIVAL &amp; DEPARTURE TIME

- Rooms are available for new arrivals from 1200hrs.
- Check out time on the day of departure is 1000hrs

## EARLY CHECK-IN

- No charges for check-in between 1000 Hours to 1200 Hours-Subject to room availability.
- Early check in between 0600 Hours to 1000 Hours - Half day charges (50% of the Room Rate)-Subject to room availability.
- Early check in before 0600 Hours would attract full day charge (100% of the room rate)-In this case would request you to kindly book from the previous night for guaranteed room at check in.

## LATE CHECKOUT

- No charges for checkout between 1000 Hours to 1200 Hours, subject to room availability.
- Late check out between 1200 Hours to 1600 Hours - Half day charges (50% of the Room Rate).
- Late check out after 1600 Hours would attract full day charge (100% of the room rate).

## DAY USE

- Day room rate (subject to room availability) is at fifty percent (50%) of prevailing Rates.
- Check out for day use is 1800hrs. Check out after 1800hrs is subject to the full room rate.
