## LOCATION

We are located at North Coast of Mombasa, 20 minutes' drive from the City Centre. Set on nine acres of colorful tropical gardens overlooking the Indian Ocean, the hotel captures the traditional Swahili architecture with two swimming pools, white sandy beach and soothing breeze against the tropical palm trees.

## ROOMS &amp; APARTMENTS

Escape to paradise with our 72 stylish hotel rooms and 10 spacious two-bedroom apartments, each offering stunning views of our sparkling pool and lush, tropical gardens.
