## Adapter

Each room comes equipped with a universal connection plug board. In case you still need an adapter, please contact reception.

## Animals

For your own safety as well as the well-being of the animals, it is not allowed to feed any animals in the hotel. Please don't leave food of any kind outside the room. Remember to keep your doors &amp; windows closed as monkeys are curious.

## Banking/ ATMs

There are several banks and ATMs in Watamu. Please note that sometimes EC Cards will not be accepted by the ATM. Therefore please have your passport available for direct withdrawal at the counter.

## Beach Vendors

Your security is important to us. Please don't book any boat trips, excursions or safaris with people you met on the beach. Even though their prices often seem relatively cheap, their offer usually doesn't include insurance or a guarantee for a safe and carefree itinerary. Should you still opt to make such a booking, please be advised to never pay in advance and please inform our reception of your absence.

## Cinema

Our open air cinema, located at Bazaar, runs every Friday, Saturday and Sunday evening. It requires headphone rentals at a small fee. Please see the movie lists on our social media page @templepointresort or on our InfoDesk at the reception area.

## Air Conditioner

To activate your AC, please insert your room key card into the tray underneath it and use the available remote control. In order to protect the environment, please keep your AC switched off while you are outside of the room.

You can reach the beach on a small pathway next to House 1. At low tide you can make your way to the long beach. Please take note that it is strictly forbidden to take corals or mussels from the Marine Park with you.

## Sun

The sunlight at the equator is very intense! Please protect yourself as well as your children from it. It is advisable to wear sunscreen of at least SPF 20, sunglasses, hat and a tshirt - especially on the beach. Also please don't forget to drink enough water throughout the day.

## Swimming in the Creek

The hotel is located at the Mida Creek, an arm of the ocean that reaches deep into the country. Especially during low

and high tide, there can be strong currents. Therefore, please don't swim out too far. Our water sports team will be happy to provide you with the best times for water sports.
