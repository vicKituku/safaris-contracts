## BOOKING TERMS

All provisional bookings are given an automatic expiry date (normally 2 weeks from booking date). You are required to confirm the booking prior to the expiry date to avoid automatic release of the booking. If you require more time to confirm the booking, the expiry date may be extended on request. Bookings are confirmed upon receipt of a 50% deposit of the total reservation cost. The balance is payable in full 45 days prior to arrival.
