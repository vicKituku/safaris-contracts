## ACTIVITIES

Kipekee offers a dynamic range of safari adventures and cultural experiences:

-  Game  Drives -  Private  guided  excursions  tailored  to  your  interests,  whether  tracking  the  Big  Five  or witnessing the wildebeest migration.
-  Maasai Boma Visit - Engage with Maasai tradition through beadwork, dance, and visits to a traditional village.
-  Bonfire  Evenings -  Gather  under  the  stars  around  a  glowing  campfire  enjoying  drinks,  snacks,  Maasai stories, and performances.
-  Bush Breakfast &amp; Dinner - Delight in serene meals surrounded by nature-sunrise breakfasts or romantically lit dinners in the bush.
-  Sundowner - Sip your drink against the backdrop of an African sunset, with light bites in a classic safari tradition.
-  Hot Air Balloon Safari - Drift over the Mara at dawn, then enjoy a champagne breakfast in the bush-an iconic, unforgettable experience.
-  Walking Safari - Explore the Mara on foot with expert guides who illuminate the smaller wonders of the bush: tracks, flora, insects, and more.
