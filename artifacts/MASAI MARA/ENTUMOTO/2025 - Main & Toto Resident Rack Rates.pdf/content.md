## POLICIES

## Reservation Policy

To secure a booking, a 30% deposit must be paid within 10 days, and upon receipt of the deposit, a c rmation voucher will be issued. For credit facilities or unique contracts, a separate application is required. Full payment must be completed prior to the clients' arrival, and failure to make payment within one week of the accommodation date may result in the loss of the booking.

## Cancellation Policy

In the event of a booking postponement, your payment can be held for a future booking without penalty fees. However, if you cancel your booking more than 8 weeks before your arrival, the 30% deposit will be forfeited. If you cancel within 4-6 weeks of your travel, the cancellation charges will amount to 60% of the total cost of your safari. Cancellation within 3 weeks of the travel date will result in being charged the full amount of the safari. If you fail to travel or join your safari after it has started, or if you leave before its completion, no refund will be issued.
