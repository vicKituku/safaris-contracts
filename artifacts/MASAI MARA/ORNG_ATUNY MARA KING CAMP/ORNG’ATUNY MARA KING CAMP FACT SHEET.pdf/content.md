## FACT SHEET

## LOCATION

Orng'atuny Mara King Camp is located at Masai Mara Ololaimutiek Village, MendugiiHill, 3.5 Km from Oloolaimutia Centre. The camp is right inside Mendungii Hill, an area considered important for both wildlife and indigenous trees.Located in the middle of an African Bush,Orngatuny Mara King Camp is the most picturesque location in the Mara where you can relax and unwind while safely enjoying wildlife.
