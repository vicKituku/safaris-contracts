Ishara is the Swahili word for 'sign', and in this majestic part of the world, nature invites you to embrace each moment as a sign.

Uniquely situated in one of the most sought-after locations in the Masai Mara National Reserve, Ishara is an immersive luxury experience steeped in intense African splendour. From the moment you  step  across  our  threshold,  you  will  enter  a  world  of  breathtaking  landscapes  and  mindful hospitality, where adventure melds with sustainable luxury and the wild heart of nature beats in harmony with serene comfort.

Meticulously  reimagined  in  every  aspect,  Ishara  beckons  with  a  blend  of  the  familiar  and  the magical, offering a gateway to not just witness, but truly live the pulse of the wilderness in a sanctuary that's been reborn, ready to unveil its secrets once more.

Ishara was built to accommodate an array of travelers with different wishes and needs. Whether you  are  a  solo  traveler  or  part  of  a  group,  and  whether  you  are  seeking  exhilaration  or relaxation, we have a breathtaking space for you to experience everything you desire. With seven River Suites, two Family Suites, one Villa Suite, and one Starbed, Ishara was created to provide joy, adventure, reflection, and serenity to all who enter.

Our  open-plan  suites  are  thoughtfully  placed  on  the  brink  of  the  wild,  amidst  the  existing ecology. Immerse yourself in the natural environment with unobstructed views of the river, forest, and expansive savanna from every corner of the spacious room and outdoor deck.

- Each River Suite (103m 2 ) offers a King or two Twin beds.
- The Villa Suite (379m 2 ) consists of two River Suites around a central lounge and dining area encompassing a private sundeck, an infinity pool and firepit. This suite accommodates up to four adults sharing.
- Each Family Suite (168m 2 ) consists of two inter-leading tents with a King or two Twin beds in one suite and two Queen beds in the other. This suite accommodates up to four adults sharing or a maximum of five for families with children (exceptions may apply).

Ishara is often visited by wildlife such as hippos, buffaloes, hyenas and even lions and leopards. Maasai  guards  will  accompany  you  as  you  move  around  camp  in  the  early  mornings,  late evenings or at night to assure safety from any wandering animals.

Our camp is best suited to host guests from the age of 8 years and above. Several of our suites and most parts of the camp are wheelchair accessible.

## Preserving our Home in the Masai Mara

We take pride in this place we call home and aim to not only preserve its natural beauty, but also  to  help  it  thrive.  Ishara  is  off-grid,  which  is  why  we  have  invested  in  a  180kWp  solarcultivating energy farm to generate renewable electricity.

An extensive rainwater harvesting system capable of collecting and storing up to 200,000 litres of fresh rainwater has been implemented at Ishara. Coupled with a water filtration plant, this supply  caters  to  the  hydro  needs  of  our  entire  camp.  A  Waste  Water  Treatment  Plant  from Bioliff Biorock© ensures that the waste can be reused safely for landscaping irrigation, outside cleaning and washing of vehicles. During harsh weather conditions or drought, a portion of the water from our harvested rainwater supply is pumped into our wildlife waterhole, guaranteeing a supply of safe drinking water for animals throughout the year.

The first of its kind in the Masai Mara, our aeroponic Tower Farm grows fresh organic produce and uses 95% less water than traditional methods. This ensures minimal land degradation and does not affect the indigenous biodiversity of the flora native to the Mara ecosystem.
