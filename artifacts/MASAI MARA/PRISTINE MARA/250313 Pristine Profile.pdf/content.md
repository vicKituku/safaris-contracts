## Pristine Mara Bay Lodge Profile

Pristine Mara Bay, is a luxury lodge, located in the remote western reaches of the Maasai Mara National Reserve.

<!-- image -->

Pristine  Mara  Bay  Lodge  is  an  exquisite haven tucked away in an exclusive location  on  one  of  the  most  beautiful  areas along  the  banks  of  the  majestic  Mara River,  in  the  Maasai  Mara.  This  top-rated lodge sits in an area that offers stunning panoramic views of the savannah and its diverse wildlife.    Our  strategically  placed lodge ensures guests are at the epicenter of the Great Migration, allowing for front-row seats to this natural spectacle as well as enjoy views of the Hippo families close by.

<!-- image -->

<!-- image -->

The  Lodge  is  known  for  its  warm  staff, excellent service, and delicious food. We offer  an  incredible  exotic  option  for  a luxury safaris, featuring rooms and a soon to  be  complete  presidential  suite  with private balconies or terraces with soaking tubs  plus  marble  bathrooms  with  rain showers,  you  can  also  choose  garden facing rooms and or river front bungalows with open bushes.

<!-- image -->

<!-- image -->

Pristine Mara Bay Lodge features elegantly  designed  rooms  that  blend seamlessly  with  the  surrounding  environment. Each room  is meticulously crafted  to  offer  comfort  and  sophistication,  with  private  decks,  en-suite  bathrooms, and modern amenities. Our aim is to provide a tranquil sanctuary where guests can unwind after a day of thrilling safaris.

There are hills that rise above the terrain, providing guests with breathtaking views of the landscape, with just 44 luxe rooms available and soon to be one presidential suite, ensuring privacy.

<!-- image -->

<!-- image -->

Complimentary  activities  include  game  drives, sundowners,  traditional  Maasai  dance  dancing classes; and Guided nature walks within the lodge. Relax on the rooftop of your room and enjoy the open vistas of the great plains teaming with wildlife  view  from  your  room,  or  soak  in  soon  to  be outdoor  hot  tubs.  There' s  also  will  be  a  fitness center and a spa.

We invite you to stay with us and experience our African  Hospitality  right  in  the  wilderness  of  the Masai Mara National Reserve.
