## 2025 CONTRACT RATES AND AGREEMENT

Pristine Mara Bay Lodge, is a 5-star luxury lodge, located in the remote western reaches of the Maasai Mara National Reserve. Perfectly situated on the banks of a tributary of the Mara River while touching the earth lightly and floating across the landscape, this pristine section, allows you to experience an intimate and exclusive Maasai Mara safari.

Comprising of 44 nostalgically elegant, and very spacious guest standard suites, that offer natural light, an enormous bed, bath, an indoor shower. Of these are 4 Family Suites that offer the addition of a smaller children's bedroom.

Coming in 2026 is one presidential suite offering adventurous families the unique opportunity to explore the African bush together.

## Terms and Conditions

Upon confirmation of a booking 30% deposit must be paid to secure the booking within 7 days. At least 60 days prior to arrival the remaining payment must be cleared. In the event that full payment is not received within the specified time, Pristine Mara Bay Lodge may at its discretion terminate this agreement forthwith and thereafter shall have no obligation to provide service(s) to the clients.

Block bookings confirmed and a deposit of 30% not paid within 14 days will automatically be released. The final payment should be cleared at least 60 days prior to clients' arrival at camp. Management reserves the right to accept block bookings.
