Children below 8 years of age will not be allowed at the camp unless their booking is on 'exclusive use' basis and in which case the children should always be under their parents/guidance supervision. Children below 3-12 years (when permitted to stay) will be charged 50% of adult sharing rate

## Cancellation Policy:

- Non-refundable 30% deposit required to confirm the booking 14 days after confirmation
- 100 % Payment of the total Invoice is required 60 days to the date of arrival for high and peak season
- 100 % Payment of the total Invoice is required 30 days to the date of arrival for low season
- Any cancellation 60 days to your date of arrival leads to 65% refund of the total invoice.
- Any cancellation 45 Days to your date of arrival leads to 45 % refund of the total invoice.
- Any cancellation 30 days to your date of arrival leads to 25% refund of the total invoice.
- No refund for any cancellation below 30 days

(Consult the camp for credit on no refund bookings)
