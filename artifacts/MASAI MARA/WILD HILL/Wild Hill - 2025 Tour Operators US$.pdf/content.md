## Wild Hill at a glance

Wild Hill is an exclusive use property with five luxurious suites, each designed with the utmost attention to detail and comfort. Named simply after numerical Maasai numbers, each suite has a spacious lounge, with a coffee station, mini bar and fireplace, huge bedrooms with baths overlooking the vast plains. There are indoor and outdoor showers and verandah that stretch across the front of the suites, making the most of the views below.

## The Guest Suites

## Nabo [Nah - boh]:

The family suite, with two ensuite bedrooms and a shared living area set inbetween massive boulders.

## Are [Ah- reh]:

Tucked down on the edge of the hill, in a colobus grove, set inbetween ancient fig trees.

## Uni [Oo-ni]:

Further along the ridge, looking over the rocky outcrops that frame the wide open views.

## Ungwan [Ong - won]:

Set on the edge of the ridge with grassy knowles and looking over the rocky outcrops and vast plains beyond.
