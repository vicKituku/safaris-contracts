## 1.Experiences - Adventure &amp; Exploration  ️ 🌄

Game Drives: Explore the vast Maasai Mara with expert local guides in open-air safari vehicles, maximizing visibility and comfort. These drives often bring encounters with Africa's Big Five and the Great Migration, providing unforgettable moments .

Behind the Wheel Experience: A unique, thrilling option where guests take control of a safari vehicle, accompanied by an experienced guide. This hands-on experience is ideal for safari enthusiasts. Price: $725 for a half day

Golf in the Wild: A one-of-a-kind golfing experience where guests can aim to hit biodegradable golf balls 400 feet into the wild on a map of Africa. Price:* $64 for three shots; $179 for the full golf experience .

Kids' Activities: Ziplining, Masai warrior training, and indoor camping make this lodge perfect for families. Babysitting services are available for parents.

Sundowners &amp; Marshmallows: Guests can enjoy a beautiful sunset while roasting marshmallows around a campfire.

Helicopter Excursions &amp; Orphanage Visits: Experience aerial views of the Mara or visit the Olmalaika Orphanage to give back to the local community.

## 2. Wildlife - Safari at its Best: The Heart of Masai Mara

🐾 Highest Concentration of Wildlife:

Located in the Maasai Mara National Reserve, Spirit of the Masai Mara offers encounters with the Big Five and, during certain seasons, the great Migration.

No Fences - Open Migration Area:

The lodge sits in a private conservancy forming part of an open wildlife corridor connecting the Maasai Mara and Serengeti.

- 💧 Private Waterhole Outside the Villas:

Each villa has views of a private waterhole where guests can observe wildlife from the comfort of their rooms.

## 3) Accommodation - Luxury in the Wild 🏡✨

🏡 Safety and Luxury Combined:

Each villa offers modern luxury with 24-hour hot water, spacious bathtubs, and glass-fronted windows, providing stunning views of the waterhole. Villas are ideal for families, accommodating two adults and three children. 🌅 Glass-Fronted Villas - Stunning Views:

Uninterrupted views of wildlife at the waterhole from your villa.

## 4) Family-Owned, Family-Run - Authentic Local Experience 👨👩👧👦

👑 Owned by the Local Masai Family:

Spirit of the Masai Mara is owned and operated by a local Masai family, ensuring an authentic experience.

- 👩🌾 Employing Local Families:

The lodge provides employment for local families and focuses on empowering women through roles in guest relations, guiding, and other operations.

- 💪 Supporting Local Communities:

By staying at Spirit of the Masai Mara, guests contribute to local schools and community projects, particularly those focused on women's empowerment.

## 5) Safety &amp; Security - Seamless Comfort in the Wilderness

️ Rural Environment with Secure Surroundings:

Set on 56,000 acres of pristine conservation land, Spirit of the Masai Mara provides a perfect balance between wilderness and security.

💫 Immersed in Local Culture:

Guests can experience Masai culture through traditional dress, warrior training, bush walks, and animal tracking with expert guides.

## 6) No Malaria Zone - Peace of Mind for Families 👨👩👧👦

️ High-Altitude Location:

The lodge's altitude creates a malaria-free zone, ideal for families.

🏥 Access to Medical Facilities:

Nearby clinics and AMREF emergency services ensure that medical help is readily available.

With a unique combination of luxury, culture and unparalleled wildlife Spirit Of The Masai Mara offers an immersive, once in a lifetime safari experience perfect for families, adventures and luxury seekers alike
