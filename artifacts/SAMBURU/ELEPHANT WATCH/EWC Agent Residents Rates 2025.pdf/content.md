## ELEPHANT       WATCH Camp &amp; Safaris

Explore SENTIENCE in the Wild with Warrior-Nomads Hosted by expert guides from the local community, our unique focus  on  'elephant-watching'  in  Kenya's  exquisite  Samburu reserve introduces guests to the mysterious world of elephant society and sentience. Each newborn calf belongs to a family whose history  we  can  be  trace  back  four  generations  in  this 1000-strong population of individually-known elephants.

<!-- image -->

Thanks to our partners at  research  NGO  Save  The  Elephants, our  profound  understanding  of  the  bonds  between  cousins and  daughters,  or  late  aunts  and  great  grandmothers  -  that continue to influence relationships between elephant families today - offers fascinating and knowledgeable portraits of these remarkable elephants' lives.  It's a life-changing experience.

These intimate encounters are further enhanced by the insights  of  the  Samburu  people  whose  charm  and  irreverent sense  of  fun  guarantee  an  unforgettable  adventure.  With  a wildlife-tolerant  culture  that  allows  for  peaceful  co-existence with  elephants  and  other  wildlife,  the  nomads  are  crucial stewards of the land and are now emerging as the new faces of conservation.
