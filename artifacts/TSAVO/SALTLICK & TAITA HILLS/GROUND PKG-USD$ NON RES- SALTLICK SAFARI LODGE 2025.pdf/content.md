## 2025 Non- Résidents Ground Package

Salt Lick Safari Lodge has long been a cornerstone of Kenya's hospitality industry, renowned as one of the country's premier lodges and earning its place among the World's Most Photographed Lodges. Drawing travelers seeking unparalleled accommodations, this luxurious safari retreat is nestled in the picturesque Taita Hills Wildlife Sanctuary. From its vantage point, guests are treated to breathtaking views of a bustling waterhole and the expansive Tsavo plains, creating an unforgettable safari experience.

## ONE NIGHT PACKAGE!

The One Night Package at Salt Lick Safari Lodge offers an all-inclusive experience combining accommodation, meals, transport, game drives, and a unique cultural visit. Here's a detailed breakdown of what the package includes:

## PACKAGE DETAILS:

## Accommodation &amp; Meals

- One Night Full-Board Accommodation : Enjoy a comfortable stay with all meals included (breakfast, lunch, and dinner).
- All-Day Drinking Water : Complimentary drinking water available throughout your stay.

## Transport

- Return Transfers in Voi : Private transfers from the Voi train station to the resort and back.

## Activities

- Two Game Drives : Experience wildlife up close with two scheduled game drives in the sanctuary.
- Sanctuary Entry Fees : All necessary entry fees to the sanctuary are covered.
- Viewing of wildlife at the waterhole thought the tunnel
- WW1 Talk &amp; Artifacts Museum Visit : Engage in a historical World War I talk and explore the artifacts museum at the resort.

## WHAT TO EXPECT

## Day 1

- Arrival &amp; Check-in : After your train journey to Voi, you'll be transferred to the Salt Lick Safari Lodge.
- Lunch : Enjoy a hearty lunch at Taita Hills Safari Resort &amp; Spa
- WW1 Talk &amp; Museum Visit : Attend the talk and visit the artifacts museum in the afternoon
- Game Drive : Embark on your first game drive to explore the local wildlife.
- Dinner : Return to the lodge for dinner.

## Day 2

- Morning Game Drive : Start your day with an early game drive.
- Breakfast : Have breakfast upon returning to the resort.
- Leisure Time : Enjoy some leisure time at the resort before checkout.
- Viewing of wildlife at the waterhole thought the tunnel
- Check-out &amp; Return : Check out and be transferred back to Voi Train Station for your return train journey.

## Inclusions Summary

- One night full-board accommodation
- All-day drinking water
- Return transfers in Voi
- Two game drives within Taita Hills Wildlife Sanctuary.
- Sanctuary entry fees
- Viewing of wildlife at the waterhole through the tunnel
- WW1 talk and visit to the artifacts museum
