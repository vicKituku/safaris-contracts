## T aita Hills &amp; Salt Lick Wildlife Resort Ltd. Terms &amp; Conditions (Non- Residents)

## 1. Child Policy

- Child from infant to age 4yrs sharing with 1 or 2 adults - FOC at Taita Hills Safari Resort &amp; Spa
- Children age 5 yrs to 11.99 yrs sharing with 1 or two adults - 50% of the adult sharing (half twin) rate per child
- Maximum three guests per room
- Children age 5 yrs to 11.99 yrs in own room (minimum 2, maximum 3) - 75% of the adult sharing (half twin) rate per child
- If children are younger than 12 yrs, they should share connecting rooms (subject to availability)
- For safety reasons, children under the age of 5 years are not permitted at Salt Lick Safari Lodge
- Please see occupancy rules below;

## 2. Occupancy Rules;

Salt Lick Safari Lodge Maximum 3 adults or 2 adults and 1 child (adults and children)

- Subject to availability of triple rooms.
- Interconnecting rooms are NOT available at Salt Lick Safari Lodge

Taita Hills Safari Resort &amp; Spa Maximum 3 adults or 2 adults and 1 child (adults and children)

- Subject to availability of triple rooms
- Interconnecting rooms are available at Taita Hills Safari Resort &amp; Spa.
- The Suites- Maximum 2 adults and 1 child
