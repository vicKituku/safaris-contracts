## 2025 Non- Résidents Ground Package

Located at the main entrance to the Taita Hills Wildlife Sanctuary, Taita Hills Safari Resort &amp; Spa welcomes guests with its inviting stone building comprising 59 spacious Resort Rooms and 2 Executive Suites, each boasting a private viewing balcony for an immersive safari experience.

The Resort holds historical significance as a heritage-built German fortress during World War 1, adding a unique dimension to its character. Visitors can explore this rich history through a museum onsite, which proudly displays World War 1 memorabilia, offering insights into the heritage of Taita Taveta County.
