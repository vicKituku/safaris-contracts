## Child Policy

- Child from infant to age 4yrs sharing with 1 or 2 adults -FOC at Taita Hills Safari Resort &amp; Spa
- Children aged 5 yrs. to 11.99 yrs. sharing with 1 or two adults -50% of the adult sharing (half twin) rate per child.
- -Maximum three guests per room
- Children aged 5 yrs. to 11.99 yrs. in own room (minimum 2, maximum 3) -75% of the adult sharing (half twin) rate per child.
- -If children are younger than 12 yrs., they should share connecting rooms (subject to availability)
- For safety reasons, children under the age of 5 years are not permitted at Salt Lick Safari Lodge
- Please see occupancy rules below;

Taita Hills Safari Resort &amp; Spa -Maximum 3 adults or 2 adults and 1 child (adults and children)

- Sanctuary entry fees do not apply for stays at Taita Hills Safari Resort &amp; Spa unless guests enter the sanctuary for game activities.

## Tour Leaders

- Tour Leaders will be on complimentary based on 1 per 15 full paying pax provided it is pre-booked and confirmed to a maximum of 3 tour leaders.
- Tour Leader for groups less than 15 full paying pax will enjoy 50% of single or twin based rate.  This must be pre-booked and confirmed.
- All other supplements &amp; sanctuary fees apply to tour leaders irrespective of passenger numbers in the group.

6

## Agent / FAM Trip Rates

- Agents Rates /FAM trips will enjoy 50% of single contracted rate for single room use per night, and 75% of double contracted rate for double/twin room stay.
- All other supplements &amp; Sanctuary fees apply to agents.
- The Agent Rates do not apply during the Christmas &amp; Easter dates.
- Agents' Rates are on a request basis &amp; subject to availability.

## Group Discounts

- 30-49 guests 5% off applicable contract rates
- 50-99 guests -7.5% off applicable contract rates
- 100 guests and over -10% off applicable contract rates
- For every 15 pax paying, 1 pax sharing goes free up to a maximum of 3 pax per group booking.
- Maximum 4 single rooms per group
- -Single rooms of more than 4 pay sole use of double/twin room rate.

## Day Room &amp; Check-In/ Checkout Policy

- Check-in time 1200hrs
- Checkout time 1000hrs
- Day rooms can be booked for 50% of the double/single room rate for use until 17h00 inclusive of lunch.
- Day rooms are subject to the same occupancy rules as overnight rules.
- Departure from the room after 17h00hrs is charged at full overnight rate.

## Walk-in Meals / Additional Meals (as per COVID-19 guidelines and protocols)

- Hot Buffet breakfast at US$ 25 per person
- Hot Buffet lunch at US$ 25 per person
- Buffet dinner at US$ 30 per person
- Additional picnic box meal (breakfast, lunch, or dinner) US$ 20 per person
- Children pay 50% of the above meals rate.

## Half Board Reduction

- HB reduction US$ 5 per adult /child per night

1. All terms and conditions apply.

- -All booking vouchers must be sent to reservations office for confirmation of availability and subsequent reservations of the same upon availability.

- -for Salt Lick Safari Lodge; reservations@saltlicksafarilodge.com

## 4. Payments Terms:

Cash Payments: All bookings must be accompanied by a travel agency voucher and paid before the arrival date. Cheques will not be accepted.

## 5. Applicability:

This contract is valid for non-residents and for leisure business only. A valid copy of Identification will be required at check-in. These rates are not for onward distribution to 3 rd  party on-line travel/hotel booking websites.

## 6. Cancelation and No-Show

If the Agent cancels the reservation the Resorts has the right to apply the following cancellation conditions:

- -30 to 16 days before arrival date - 20% loss of the total amount of the accommodation booked.
- -15 to 8 days before arrival date - 40% loss of the total amount of the accommodation booked.
- -7 to 3 days before arrival date - 75% loss of the total amount of the accommodation booked.
- -2 days- to date of arrival - 100% loss of the total amount of the accommodation booked.

## 7. Variation in rates, and/or taxes and/or service charge

The Resort and Lodge may at its discretion, by not less than one month's notice in writing to the Agent alter the contracted rates. In the event  of  any  appreciation  of  the  Kenya  shilling  of  over  5%  vis-à-vis  the  currency  in  which  the  contract  is  drawn  or  any  changes  in government taxes, training levy or service charge, The Resort and Lodge reserves the right to adjust these rates accordingly, from the date of such changes and/or any variations.

## 8. Confidentiality

The Agent confirms and agrees that this Agreement is confidential to the Agent and Taita Hills &amp; Salt Lick Wildlife Resort Ltd and the Agent shall not in any circumstances divulge or permit to divulge this agreement or its contents to any other person.

Amendments to this agreement shall be valid only if made in writing and signed by the Agent and The Resort. The waiver of any of the terms on any occasion shall not be deemed a waiver of such terms and conditions for any further occasion. ## 10. Force Majeure

Acts of nature may affect the ability of the Resort to provide accommodation as stated in this agreement.  In this case, the liability of the Resort is limited to the refund of amounts paid.

## 11. Overbooking and Rerouting

In the unlikely instance that the Resort is unable to accommodate guests on a confirmed reservation, the Resort reserves the right to ask the Agent to consider revising the routing of the itinerary or amend the arrival date.
