1. The **methods of payment** offered to the Tour Operator/Hotel are as follows:

1. Green fees for 18 or 9 holes should be paid at the Golf Reception, by presenting the Tour Operator/Hotel greenfee voucher on the day of play. A sample of the Tour Operator/Hotel voucher must be provided to VRL.
2. If a valid greenfee voucher is not presented at the reception, VRL reserves the right to prevent the client from playing until the payment is received.
3. The invoices of the received vouchers will be sent to the Tour Operator/Hotel every 30 days and invoices must be paid within the following 15 days period.

If this period is exceeded. VRL reserves the right to cancel any further bookings until outstanding payments are settled.

1. The **cancellation** procedure for all starting times booked in this category will be as follows:

1. For bookings made more than 14 days before the day of play, the cancellation is 7 days. If cancellation is made after this date is passed, a penalty of 30% of the Tour Operator/Hotel green fee rate will be charged and the starting times will revert to VRL, if cancellation is not made two (2) days before the day of play, a penalty of the full Tour Operator/Hotel green fee rate will be charged.
2. For bookings made less than 14 days before the day of play, the cancellation is 4 days. If cancellation is made after this date  is passed, a penalty of 50% of the Tour Operator/Hotel green fee rate will be charged and the starting times will revert to VRL, if cancellation is not made two (2) days before the day of play, a penalty of the full Tour Operator/Hotel greenfee rate will be charged.
3. For bookings made within 7 days of the day of play, the cancellation is 48 hours. If cancellation is made after this date is passed, a penalty of the full Tour Operator/Hotel green fee rate will be charged.
4. For bookings made within 48 hours, there will be no penalty for cancellation.

1. The **Golf Course Rules and Etiquette** are as follows:

1. We encourage guests to show a valid handicap certificate at the golf reception before playing.
2. It is compulsory that all players are accompanied by a caddie on the golf course which will be charged at the rate of KES 1,000 for 18 holes.
3. If a guest rents a golf cart, the compulsory caddie fee is not applicable. A guest may still pay for a caddie to accompany the players on the course at their own discretion.
4. Each Player must have his or her own golf bag with their own clubs.
5. No sharing of golf bags or clubs is permitted on the golf course at any time.
6. All players must wear appropriate golf footwear on the golf course at all times.
7. A buggy may carry a maximum of two (2) players and (2) golf bags and no person under the age of 17 may drive a buggy hired at Vipingo Ridge Golf Club.
8. All players are responsible for any damage to buggies during their round and must full reimburse VRL all costs associated with the damages.
9. All players must replace divots on the fairways, repair pitch marks on the greens and rake bunkers after play.
10. In order to maintain our high standards of dress, members &amp; guests are expected to dress accordingly. No swimwear, gym wear, sportswear, denim jeans, T shirts without collars and training shoes will be allowed on the golf course at any time. Players are expected to wear socks with appropriate golf footwear on the golf course. Shirts must also be tucked in to the waist band of Bermuda length shorts and trousers at all times on the golf course.
11. **Note: It is the responsibility of the Tour Operator/Hotel to inform their clients of the above requirements. If clients are refused permission to play because they do not conform with the above rules, the green fee will be charged.**
12. **Please help us to avoid difficult situations by informing your clients and staff clearly of our rules in advance** **.**

**BAD WEATHER POLICY**

These are conditions for the various situations that arise in due to bad weather:

1. **THE COUSRSE WILL BE CLOSED FOR THE FOLLOWING REASONS**

1. flooded greens
2. thunder and lightening
3. if the conditions are bad enough in any way as to be considered dangerous to the players or damaging to the golf course. – i.e., fog or flooding.
4. In bad weather, a final decision will be made at 07h00am each morning and hourly after this. The Tour Operator/Hotel should contact the reception of the Club to be informed of the condition of the course
5. No NO-SHOW green fee will be charged

1. **THE COURSE IS CLOSED AFTER THE CLIENT HAS STARTED HIS/HER ROUND**

- 2.1. In the above situations the following conditions will apply:

1. If the client has played no more than 5 holes/full cash or voucher refund will be given, depending on the method of green fee payment.
2. If the client has played more than 5 holes and not more than 12 holes, a 9-hole cash or voucher refund will be given depending on the method of green fee payment.

NB: If either of the above procedures are used, the necessary adjustments to the client’s voucher must be made and the appropriate Tour Operator/Hotel will be notified. The adjusted voucher will be signed by a member of the Vipingo Ridge Golf Resort management  and the client.

If clients request an alternative starting time, a credit 9/18hole voucher may be issued by the Vipingo Ridge Golf Resort Reception if there is a suitable starting time available within the next 48 hours.

1. **THE COURSE IS OPEN BUT THE USE OF BUGGIES IS NOT PERMITTED**

3.1 In this situation, the following conditions will apply:

a.) not being allowed to use a buggy is not considered a reason for a client to NO-SHOW and a relative NO-SHOW charge will be made. However, if a client can present a valid medical certificate to show that he/she requires a buggy to play the NO-SHOW charge will be waived.

**All decisions concerning the above conditions will be made by Vipingo Ridge Golf Resort and it is up to the Tour Operator/Hotel to inquire with the Golf Reception if they have any doubt as to the procedure on any particular day.**
