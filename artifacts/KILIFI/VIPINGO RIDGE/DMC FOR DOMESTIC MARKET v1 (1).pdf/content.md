In the event of a valid cancellation notification being received, the following terms will apply:

1. If  cancellation  is  made  between  20  -  15 days a penalty of 25% of the booking will be charged.
2. If  cancellation  is  made  between  14  -  10 days a penalty of 40% of the booking will be charged.
3. If cancellation is made between 9 - 5 days a  penalty  of  60%  of  the  booking  will  be charged.

## 9. Villa and Hotel Room Occupancies

The villas at Vipingo Ridge are all different sizes and have a different number of rooms. Please note that the maximum occupancy in each room is 2 people (a child 3 years and under can be accommodated in a cot in each of the rooms in the villa or hotel rooms). Therefore  a  1-bedroom  villa  can  accommodate  a maximum of 2 adults + an infant under 3 years, a 2 bedroom villa  can  accommodate  a  maximum  of  4 adults + 2 infants under 3 years etc. The one-bedroom standard  room  option  may  be  a  room  in  a  larger villa, which is self-contained and separated from the other rooms which may be occupied by other guests. These  occupancy  restrictions  also  include  the  selfcontained standard rooms.

## 10. Child Policy

Vipingo Ridge Ltd. welcomes children of all ages.

1. Infants are defined as 3 years of age and below and can be accommodated in a cot as covered in 9 above.
2. There is no reduction in room rates for children.
3. Children are defined for the purposes of room occupancy  restrictions  as  between  4  and  18 years of age and are counted as a hotel guest as covered in 9 above.
4. Adults for the purposes of occupancy restrictions are any guest over the age of 18.
5. Children should be always accompanied by an adult at Vipingo Ridge.
