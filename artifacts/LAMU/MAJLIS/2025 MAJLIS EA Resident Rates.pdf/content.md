- Bookings will be considered confirmed upon receipt of a booking voucher from the tour operator.

- Full balance of payment is required 30 days prior to guests' arrival.
- For last minute booking, full payment is due upon confirmation.
- The Majlis Lamu may, at its discretion, not honor a booking if the deposit &amp; full payment is not received prior to arrival.
- We reserve the right to make amendments to the rates in the event of any increase in taxes, levies, statutory charges or any other input costs.

## Cancellation &amp; Reduction In Numbers Policy :

In the event of any cancellation or reduction in numbers made.

- Between 60 - 35 days prior to date of stay 30% of the total cost will be charged.
- Between 34 - 20 days prior to date of stay 50% of the total cost will be charged.
- Between 19 - Arrival date 100% of the total cost will be charged.
- No Show will be charged @ 1 night's accommodation rate
