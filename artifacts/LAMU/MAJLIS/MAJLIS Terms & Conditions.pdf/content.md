## Child Policy:

- Children from 0 - 3 years are free of charge.
- Children from 4 - 11 years sharing with 2 adults pay 50% of the Adult rate
- Children of 4 - 11 years in their own room pay 75% of room rate (maximum 3 children)
- Single adult sharing with a child of 4 - 11 years, pay Single Room Rate + 50% of Adult rate (for the child)

## Cancellation &amp; Reduction In Numbers Policy :

In the event of any cancellation or reduction in numbers made.

- Between 90 - 60 days prior to date of stay 20% of the total cost will be charged.
- Between 60 - 35 days prior to date of stay 30% of the total cost will be charged.
- Between 34 - 20 days prior to date of stay 50% of the total cost will be charged.
- Between 19 - Arrival date ( No Show) 100% of the total cost will be charged.
- No Show will be charged @ 1 night's accommodation rate.
