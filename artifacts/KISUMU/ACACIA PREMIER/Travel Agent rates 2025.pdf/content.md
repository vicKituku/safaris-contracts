## LEISURE RATE CONTRACT 2025

This CONTRACT is made and entered by and between the Acacia Premier Hotel Kisumu , hereinafter referred to as the HOTEL and the Travel Agent hereinafter referred to as the CUSTOMER.

## Article 1 GENERAL

The HOTEL hereby agrees to lodge the CUSTOMER'S, guests at special or discounted rates (hereinafter referred to as LEISURE RATES ) and the CUSTOMER hereby accepts the HOTEL'S offer for lodging its employees or guests traveling to Kisumu for leisure travel in any one of the rooms described herein and agrees to book such employees or guests their stay at the HOTEL'S rooms.

## Article 2 DURATION OF THE AGREEMENT

The present CONTRACT shall remain valid for a period commencing on 1 st January 2025 and ending on December 31, 2025 unless sooner terminated in accordance with the provisions stipulated below.  It shall not be automatically extended and is to be re-negotiated in December 2025, for the following year.

- 4.3 The HOTEL reserves the right to adjust the room rates indicated above upon prior notice by giving advance notice to the CUSTOMER .

## Article 5 RESERVATIONS, CANCELLATION, CHECK IN AND CHECK OUT

- 5.1 All reservations should be made directly through the HOTEL Reservation Department.
- 5.2 The Reservation Department of the HOTEL can be contacted through the addresses indicated below:
- +254 709850000

E-mail: reservations@acaciapremier.com

- 5.3 The CUSTOMER or its guest may cancel its or his reservation subject to the following conditions:
- a. The HOTEL shall impose 100% charge of entire booking, if cancellation made two days before the date of arrival.
- b. The HOTEL shall impose 75% charge of entire booking, if cancellation made four days before the date of arrival.
- c. The HOTEL shall impose 25% charge of entire booking, if cancellation made seven days before the date of arrival.
- d. The HOTEL shall waive charges for entire booking, if cancellation made Fourteen days before the date of arrival.
- e. Rooming list to be provided Fourteen days before the arrival date.
- f. Advance payments for the booking needs to be made at the confirmation of the group.
- g. Any booking without advance payment or rooming list as per above will be cancelled from the hotel's booking system.
- 5.4 A guest shall be entitled to check in starting from 2:00p.m.local time and checkout 10:00 am on departure date.
- 5.5 Subject to availability, the HOTEL may give a room for guests who want to check in early at an extra charge amounting to 50% of the room's rent for the first day.
- 5.6 Rent shall be charged by the HOTEL for unused days in the event of an early check out.
- 5.7 Late check outs made between 12:00 noon and 6:00 P. M. on the day of departure shall entail a charge amounting to 60% of the room rack rate for one night.
- 5.8 Late checkout made later than 06:00 P.M on the date of departure shall be charged 100% of the room charge of the previous night.

## Article 7 TERMS AND CONDITIONS

## 7.1 UNAVAILABILITY OF ROOMS

In the event where the HOTEL cannot accommodate a guest with confirmed reservation due to fortuitous circumstance or factors beyond its control, the HOTEL shall provide the following:

- a. Accommodation at a comparable hotel located as closely as possible for the first night of displacement;
- b. One complimentary round trip ground transportation between the Hotel and the alternate hotel.
- c. The HOTEL will make the necessary arrangements for forwarding the displaced guest's telephone messages and mail.
- d. The CUSTOMER will receive credit for any guests displaced toward its Annual Room Night Production numbers for purposes of this agreement.
- e. The HOTEL shall have no further obligations under this Clause in the event where a room becomes available at the Hotel for the displaced guest but such guest elects not to return to the Hotel.
- f. Subject to availability, the HOTEL shall provide upgraded accommodations to a displaced guest in the event of his return to the hotel.

## 7.2   TAX AND OTHER CHANGES

The HOTEL reserves the right to change the rates in the event of any changes in the applicable government taxes, fees or assessments as well as any changes in applicable labor or service charges or fees.
