## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

## 1. RESERVATION STATUS

Reservations will be honoured with a voucher, an LPO or supporting  document  on  official  letterhead  bearing  the authorized signature advised to Rift Valley Hills Resort as set  out  in  the  Schedule  hereto. Save for manifest fraud, anything appearing to have been signed by the authorized signatory shall bind the Company. Any change of signatories should be notified to Rift Valley Hills Resort  and will take effect from the time it is received by Rift Valley Hills Resort. The exact taken up reservations by the Company along with the rooming list should be received  by Rift Valley Hills  Resort's  sales/  reservations  office  prior  to  arrival  of guests at Rift Valley Hills Resort.

## 2. RELEASE PERIOD AND CANCELLATION

Cancellations should be notified to Rift Valley Hills Resort sales/reservations  office  verbally  and  followed  by  written notice of cancellation. Cancellation will be effective on the working day on which Rift Valley Hills Resort receives the written notice. Any reservations received after 5.00pm will be  deemed  to  be  received  on  the  next  working  day. Reservations that are cancelled, reduced in length of stay or reduced in numbers are subject to Rift Valley Hills Resort prevailing  Cancellation  and  No  Show  Fees  which  are currently as follows:

- 0 -7 days, 100% of entire stay
- 8 -14 days, 50% of entire stay
- 15 -35 days, 25% of entire stay
- No show: 100% of entire stay

The number of days refers to the number of clear days between the cancellation date and the anticipated date of arrival of the guest (excluding the cancellation date and the date of arrival).

Rift Valley Hills Resort may cancel reservations in any of the following events:

- (a) If the Company fails to pay the deposit within 7 days of the due date; or
- (b) If Rift Valley Hills Resort reasonably believes that the Company's financial situation is such that the Company will not be able to honour its obligations under this agreement; or

## 3. DEPOSIT POLICY

Advance deposits where required Rift Valley Hills Resort are calculated on the total value of the booking and are payable as follows:

- 50% due if booking is made 8 -14 days prior to arrival
- 100% due if booking is made 0 hrs - 7 days prior to arrival

In the event that the Company fails to pay the deposit within 7  days  of the  due  date  (where  bookings  are  made 8-14 days prior to arrival) or within 24 hours (where bookings are made 48hrs -7 days prior to arrival) (as the case may be), Rift Valley Hills  Resort  may  treat  the  reservation  as cancelled by the Company.

4. RESERVATION GUARANTEES

Reservations  must  be  guaranteed  by  means  of  cash, cheque, credit card or inter-bank transfer in such amount as required by Rift Valley Hills Resort from time to time  and in any event not being less than the cost of one night's rate for the type of room reserved. Non-guaranteed reservations are subject to release by Rift Valley Hills Resort at 18:00hrs on the day of expected arrival.

## 5. RATE CONFIDENTIALITY

The  agreed  company  rates  are  only  valid  for  bona  fide associates of the Company and may not be revealed to, or used to book for, third parties other than in accordance with the provisions of this clause. The rates agreed here must not be displayed directly over the internet or on third party websites but may be packaged together with airfare and/or other arrangements so that the Rift Valley Hills Resort rates herein are not transparent.

6.  CHILDREN

A child in a baby cot (up 4 years old) sharing a room with an adult can stay free of charge. Children under 13 years staying in a separate room will be charged at seventy five percent (75%) of the applicable room rate &amp; 45% for a child sharing a room with adult/s.

7. ARRIVAL AND CHECKOUT TIMES

<!-- image -->

Rooms are available for new arrivals from 12.00hrs on the scheduled  arrival  date.  Check  out  time  on  the  day  of departure is 10.00hrs. Pre-booking is encouraged for early arrivals to guarantee rooms upon arrival and a pre- booking will be subject to a full overnight rate.

8.  DAY ROOM

Day room rate (subject to  room availability)  is  at  75%  of applicable  rates.  Check  out  time  for  day  room  use  is 18:00hrs.  Check-out  after  18:00hrs  is  subject  to  the  full room rate.

9.  COMPLIMENTARY ROOMS

Rift  Valley  Hills  Resort  will  subject  to  the  availability  of rooms  grant  matching  complimentary  rooms/  guests  as follows

- Each 15 paying guests in a group the 16 th person is free to a maximum of 2 single rooms.

Credit  facilities  may  be  extended  to  the  Company  upon meeting  Rift  Valley  Hills  Resort  requirements  for  such facilities.  Where  such  facilities  have  been  extended,  the Company  will,  subject  to  the  credit  approval  terms,  be permitted  make  reservations  with  a  voucher,  an  LPO  or letter of commitment bearing the authorized signature, received  prior to  any  arrival. 13. EXCHANGE RATE

Foreign currency exchange will be at Rift Valley  Hills Resort prevailing rate at the time of guest stay

14. DISPUTES

Any dispute regarding an invoice must be received by Rift Valley Hills Resort finance department within seven (7) days of the invoice date, failure to which the Company will be liable for the full invoice. The dispute will be considered and  the  Company  notified  of  Rift  Valley  Hills  Resort determination of the disputed amount. On determination of the disputed amount, only the amount in dispute may be adjusted  on  the  invoice  and  payment  will  be  due  within seven  (7)  days  of  the  adjusted  invoice. It  is  expressly agreed  and  declared  that  any  disputes,  action  or  other matters arising hereunder shall be determined in accordance with the laws and procedures of Kenya.

15. DEFAULT

Without prejudice to any other rights available to Rift  Valley Hills Resort, in the event of default in payment of undisputed invoices  and  resolved  invoices,  Rift  Valley  Hills  Resort reserves the right to refuse the Company's clients admission into and use of Rift Valley Hills Resort facilities until payment  is  made  in  full. Rates  quoted  in  this agreement are subject to the Company not defaulting as against the agreed schedule of making payments.  Where delays  in  payment  by  the  Company  occur,  the  terms  of Contract  are  in  Rift  Valley  Hills  Resort  sole  discretion subject to change and may lead to

withdrawal  of  credit  terms  previously  extended  to  the Company.

16. CONSISTENT DEFAULT

Consistent default shall constitute cause for termination of this agreement. The Company shall be considered to be in a  condition  of  CONSISTENT  DEFAULT  where  invoices submitted have not been paid in full for a period exceeding 60 days.

17.  COLLECTION FEES
