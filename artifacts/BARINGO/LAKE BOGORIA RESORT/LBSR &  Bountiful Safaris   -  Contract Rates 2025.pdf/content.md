## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

## 1.RESERVATION STATUS

Reservations will be honored with a voucher, an LPO or supporting  document  on  official  letterhead  bearing  the authorized signature advised to Lake Bogoria Spa Resort as set out in the Schedule hereto.  Save for manifest fraud, anything appearing to have been signed by the authorized signatory shall bind the Company. Any change of signatories should be notified to Lake Bogoria Spa Resort and will  take  effect  from  the  time  it  is  received  by  Lake Bogoria Spa Resort. The exact taken up reservations by the  Company  along  with  the  rooming  list  should  be received by Lake Bogoria Spa Resort's sales/ reservations office prior to arrival of guests at Lake Bogoria Spa Resort.

## 2. RELEASE PERIOD AND CANCELLATION

Cancellations  should  be  notified  to  Lake  Bogoria  Spa Resort's sales/reservations office verbally and followed by written notice of cancellation. Cancellation will be effective on  the  working  day  on  which  Lake  Bogoria  Spa  Resort receives the written notice. Any reservations received after 5.00pm will be deemed to be received on the next working day. Reservations that are cancelled, reduced in length of stay or reduced in numbers are subject to Lake Bogoria Spa Resort's prevailing Cancellation and No -Show Fees which are currently as follows:

0 -7 days, 100% of entire stay 8 -14 days, 50% of entire stay 15 -35 days, 25% of entire stay No show: 100% of entire stay

The number of days refers to the number of clear days between the cancellation date and the anticipated date of arrival of the guest (excluding the cancellation date and the date of arrival).

Lake Bogoria Spa Resort may cancel reservations in any of the following events: If the Company fails to pay the deposit within 7 days of the due date; or will not be  able to honor  its obligations

If  Lake Bogoria Spa Resort reasonably believes that the Company's financial  situation  is  such  that  the  Company under  this agreement

## 3. DEPOSIT POLICY

Advance  deposits  where  required  by  Lake  Bogoria  Spa Resort are calculated on the total value of the booking and

are payable as follows: 50% due if booking is made 8 -14 days prior to arrival 100% due if booking is made 0 hrs. - 7 days prior to arrival

In  the  event  that  the  Company  fails  to  pay  the  deposit within 7 days of the due date (where bookings are made 8-14  days  prior  to  arrival)  or  within  24  hours  (where bookings are made 48hrs -7 days prior to arrival) (as the case  may  be),  Lake  Bogoria  Spa  Resort  may  treat  the reservation as cancelled by the Company.

## 4. RESERVATION GUARANTEES

Reservations  must  be  guaranteed  by  means  of  cash, cheque, credit card or inter-bank transfer in such amount as required by Lake Bogoria Spa Resort from time to time and in any event not being less than the cost of one night's rate for the type of room reserved. Non-guaranteed reservations are subject to release by Lake Bogoria Spa Resort at 18:00hrs on the day of expected arrival.

## 5. RATE CONFIDENTIALITY

The  agreed  company  rates  are  only  valid  for  bona  fide associates of the Company and may not be revealed to, or used to  book  for,  third  parties  other  than  in  accordance with the provisions of this clause. The rates agreed here must not be displayed directly over the internet or on third party websites but may be packaged together with airfare and/or other arrangements so that the Lake Bogoria Spa Resort rates herein are not transparent.

## 6.  CHILDREN

A child in a baby cot (up 4 years old) sharing a room with an adult can stay free of charge. Children under 13 years staying in a separate room will be charged at seventy five percent (75%) of the applicable room rate &amp; 45% of single for a child sharing a room with adult/s.

## 7. ARRIVAL AND CHECKOUT TIMES

Rooms are available for new arrivals from 12.00hrs on the scheduled  arrival  date.  Check  out  time  on  the  day  of departure is 10.00hrs. Pre-booking is encouraged for early arrivals to guarantee rooms upon arrival and a prebooking will be subject to a full overnight rate.

## 8.  DAY ROOM

Day room rate (subject to room availability) is at 75% of applicable  rates.  Check  out  time  for  day  room  use  is 18:00hrs.  Check-out  after  18:00hrs  is  subject  to  the  full room rate.

## 9.  COMPLIMENTARY ROOMS

Lake Bogoria Spa Resort will subject to the availability of rooms  grant  matching  complimentary  rooms/  guests  as follows

- Each 15 paying guests in a group the 16 th  person is free to a maximum of 1 single rooms.

In the event that the Government of Kenya adjusts the prevailing or institutes a new tax regime during the duration of this agreement, Lake Bogoria Spa Resort reserves the right to adjust the rates contained in this agreement accordingly.
