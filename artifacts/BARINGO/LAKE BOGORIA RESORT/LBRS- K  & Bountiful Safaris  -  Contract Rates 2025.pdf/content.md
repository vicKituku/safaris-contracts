## 3. RELEASE PERIOD AND CANCELLATION

Cancellations  should  be  notified  to  Lake  Bogoria  Spa Kabarak's sales/reservations office verbally and followed by  written  notice  of  cancellation.  Cancellation  will  be effective on the working day on which Lake Bogoria Spa Kabarak  receives  the  written  notice.  Any  reservations received after 5.00pm will be deemed to be received on the  next  working  day.  Reservations  that  are  cancelled, reduced in length of stay or reduced in numbers are subject to Lake Bogoria Spa Kabarak prevailing

Cancellation and No-Show Fees which are currently as follows:

- 0 - 7 days, 100% of entire stay

- 8 - 14 days, 50% of entire stay

- 15 - 35 days, 25% of entire stay

No show: 100% of entire stay

The number of days refers to the number of clear days between the cancellation date and the anticipated date of arrival of the guest (excluding the cancellation date and the date of arrival).

Lake  Bogoria  Spa  Kabarak  may  cancel  reservations  in any of the following events:

If the Company fails to pay the deposit within 7 days of the due date; or

If Lake Bogoria Spa Kabarak reasonably believes that the Company's financial situation is such that the Company will not be able to honour its obligations under this agreement

## 4. DEPOSIT POLICY

Advance deposits where required by Lake Bogoria Spa Kabarak are calculated on the total value of the booking and are payable as follows:

50% due if booking is made 8 -14 days prior to arrival 100%  due  if  booking  is  made  0  hrs  -  7  days  prior  to arrival.

In  the  event  that  the  Company  fails  to  pay  the  deposit within 7 days of the due date (where bookings are made 8-14  days  prior  to  arrival)  or  within  24  hours  (where bookings are made 48hrs -7 days prior to arrival) (as the case may be), Lake Bogoria Spa Kabarak may treat the reservation as cancelled by the Company.

## 5. RESERVATION GUARANTEES

Reservations  must  be  guaranteed  by  means  of  cash, cheque, credit card or inter-bank transfer in such amount as required by Lake Bogoria Spa Kabarak from time to time and in any event not being less than the cost of one night's rate for the type of  room reserved. Nonguaranteed  reservations  are  subject  to  release  by  Lake Bogoria Spa Kabarak at 18:00hrs on the day of expected arrival.

## 6. RATE CONFIDENTIALITY

The agreed company rates are only valid for bona fide associates of the Company and may not be revealed to, or used to book for, third parties other than in accordance with the provisions of this clause. The rates agreed here must not be displayed directly over the internet or on third party websites but may be packaged together with airfare and/or other arrangements so that the Lake Bogoria Spa Kabarak rates herein are not transparent.

## 7. CHILDREN

A child in a baby cot (up 4 years old) sharing a room with an adult can stay free of charge. Children under 13 years staying in a separate room will be charged at seventy five percent (75%) of the applicable room rate &amp; 50% for a child sharing a room with adult/s.

## 8. ARRIVAL AND CHECKOUT TIMES

Rooms are available for new arrivals from 12.00hrs on the scheduled  arrival  date.  Check  out  time  on  the  day  of departure  is  10.00hrs.  Pre-booking  is  encouraged  for early  arrivals  to  guarantee  rooms  upon  arrival  and  a prebooking will be subject to a full overnight rate.

## 9. DAY ROOM

Day room rate (subject to room availability) is at 75% of applicable  rates.  Check  out  time  for  day  room  use  is 18:00hrs. Check-out after 18:00hrs are subject to the full room rate.
