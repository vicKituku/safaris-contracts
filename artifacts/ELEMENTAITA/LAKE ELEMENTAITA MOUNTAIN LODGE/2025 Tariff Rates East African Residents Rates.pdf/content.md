## CHILD RATES POLICY :

- Lake Elementaita Mountain Lodge welcomes guests of all ages.
- Children between 0 - 3yrs sleep free of charge
- Children between 4yrs to 12yrs sharing are charged 50% of the adult sharing rate. If sleeping in their own room (A minimum of 2 children) they are charged 75% of the adult sharing rate.
- Children above 12yrs are charged as adults.

*Peak Season includes all public holidays and the festive periods *Rates for exclusive use of our property are available upon request/NULL

## PROOF OF RESIDENCY :

Resident rates are applicable to residents of Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi, and Rwanda. Guests are required to provide proof of residency. Visitors without valid identification documents will be required to pay the non-resident rates.

## PROVISIONAL BOOKINGS AND PAYMENT TERMS :

A 50% deposit will be required to confirm a booking. The balance will be payable upon arrival. A confirmation voucher will only be issued once full payment has been done.

## CANCELLATION POLICY:

- 45 - 35 days prior to arrival, 20% charged
- 14 days prior to arrival, 100% charged
- Change of dates 5 days prior to arrival, 50% charged
- 34 - 15 Days prior to arrival, 50% charged
- Premature checkouts or a no show, 100% charged
