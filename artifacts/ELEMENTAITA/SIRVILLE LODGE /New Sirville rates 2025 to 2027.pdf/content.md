## ROOM BOOKING AND RELEASE PERIOD

All block bookings are to be confirmed by the agent a minimum of 30 days prior to arrival at the lodge. Rooming lists must be submitted to the lodge's reservations department a minimum of 10 days prior to arrival. Bookings made and not paid for will be released at the discretion of the lodge, without notice to the agent if payment is not received 30 days in advance of the booking.

## CANCELLATION POLICY

1. For bookings canceled or amended between 10 days from the expected date of arrival, 25% of the applicable rate per bed night will be charged.
2. For bookings canceled or amended between 9 and 48 hours before the date of arrival, 50% of the applicable rate per bed night will be charged.
3. In case of cancellation or amendment within 48 hours from the date of arrival 75% of the value of booking will be charged
4. In case of cancellation or amendment in less than 48 hours from the expected date of arrival or in case of 'NO SHOW' will attract a 100% cancellation.

## PAYMENT POLICY

- a. A 50% deposit is payable on confirmation of booking and the balance 15 days prior to the arrival of the clients. Bookings not secured by the appropriate deposit will be released automatically and without prior notification.
- b. All payments made by cheque must be cleared prior to the arrival of the clients. In the event a cheque is returned by the bank as "unpaid" for any reason the agent shall replace the payment by cash and additionally agrees to pay the returned cheque fee thereof.
- c. The agent agrees to pay any and all costs, including legal fees, court costs and any other expenses incurred by Sirville Lake Elementaita Lodge in the collection of unpaid invoices, returned cheque or default payment.
- d. Any dispute regarding an invoice must be received by Sirville Lake Elementaita Lodge accounts department within 4 days of invoicing and only amount in dispute may be deducted from the invoice and Payment due, which must be made within the specific time limit.
- e. Default of undisputed payment may result in Agent's clients being refused admission to the lodge until payment is made. Consistent default will result in cancellation of this agreement.

## VARIATION

The contractual rates mentioned in this contract are subject to change without any notice in the event of an increase in local taxes and government levies. However, every effort will be made to maintain them within the contractual period.

## TRAVEL AGENTS COMMISSION

We propose to provide a performance based commission to reward travel agents in line with their dedication to secure clients for us. The salient features of the performance based contract are elaborated below:-

- I. If  in  a  month, an agents books less than 11 double rooms his/her commission will be 10%, or if the agent brings less 3 day groups each comprising of more than 15 people the said agent shall earn a 10% commission.

An agent shall be paid 10% commission on the third day after the client has settled the bill.

- ⚫ It is the agents responsibility to ensure their performance is updated with every check-in by Sirville front desk.

- ⚫ The agents commission shall be paid no later than 3 days after the clients has fully cleared the bill.

- ⚫ If the agent is not registered for VAT and the clients pays directly to Sirville lodge - Sirville will automatically deduct VAT and catering levy at the prescribed rates.

- ⚫ The driver and the travel agents team leader will be provided food and beverages served to the group.
