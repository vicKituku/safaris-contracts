## LOCATION

Cradle Hotel is located along Kerio Road in Lodwar, Turkana County, just 5 km from Lodwar Airport. Set on a serene 16-acre property, the hotel offers a peaceful retreat with easy access to key attractions in the region. Its strategic location makes it ideal for both business and leisure travelers exploring Turkana.

## ACTIVITIES

Turkana is a region rich in culture, history, and natural beauty, offering visitors a variety of unforgettable experiences. Explore the vast Lake Turkana and its volcanic wonder, Central Island National Park, or unwind at the scenic Eliye Springs. Dive into local culture with tours of traditional Turkana villages and attend the vibrant Tobong'u Lore Cultural Fe stival. History and archaeology enthusiasts can visit the site of the famous Turkana Boy discovery and the renowned Turkana Basin Institute, a hub for research on human origins. Adventurous travelers can enjoy desert drives, camel rides, and visits to unique landmarks like the Wadachi Pyramid and the Christ the Redeemer Monument perched above Lodwar, which offers sweeping views of the town and spiritual reflection. Additional highlights include the historic Catholic Mission in Lodwar and opportunities for fishing, nature walks, and cultural storytelling.
