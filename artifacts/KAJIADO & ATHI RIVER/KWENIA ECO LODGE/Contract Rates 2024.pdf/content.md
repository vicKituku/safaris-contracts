## Child policy:

- For children under 3 years of age, sharing with adult(s) is free of charge.
- For  a minimum of 2 children between 3 and 16 years occupying their own room, the charge is 75% of the room rates as per the occupancy.
- For a child aged 3 to16 years sharing with 1 adult, the child will pay 50% of the adult in a double room.
- For children aged 3 to 16 years sharing with 2 adults, 50% of the per person adult twin rate will apply for each child.
